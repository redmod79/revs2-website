    Question¶

    Synthesizing all 25 prior revs2 studies, what is the macro-structure of the book of Revelation? How do the four en pneumati visions, the 1:19 threefold frame, the prologue-epilogue inclusio, the three judgment sequences with interludes, the sanctuary compartmental progression, the Babylon-bride mirror, the structural threads, the IC077 hybrid model, the overcomer arc, and the theophany formula progression combine into one coherent literary architecture?

    Prior Research Summary¶

    This is a synthesis study drawing on all 25 prior revs2 CONCLUSION.md files. The reference agent has read all 25 and compiled key structural findings including: IC077 hybrid model (telescoping + overlapping + escalating completeness); four en pneumati visions (Rev 1:10, 4:2, 17:3, 21:10); 1:19 threefold key (ha eides/ha eisin/ha mellei); prologue-epilogue inclusio (SP009, 9+ verbal parallels); sanctuary compartmental progression (naos x16, DOA at 15:5-8, IC155/156/157); Babylon-bride mirror (V3/V4 formula); theophany formula 3→4→5→5+ (TM081); seven beatitudes as structural spine (G3107); overcomer arc (Rev 2-3 to Rev 22); and the structural DB tally (23 Recap / 31 Linear / 25 Telescoping elements out of 1213 total). Web research confirmed four structural proposals: linear/1:19, fourfold en pneumati, sevenfold recapitulation, chiastic. Kenneth Strand's eight-vision model (AUSS 25:1, 1987) was identified but not fully accessible.

    Theophany Formula Progression: The four TM081 astrapē passages marking judgment sequence boundaries - Rev 4:5 — 3-element theophany: astrapē/phōnē/brontē — first station of TM081 theophany formula; seven lamps = seven Spirits; establishing the progression benchmark - Rev 8:1-5 — Seventh seal transition: half-hour silence (TM059) + incense offering (8:3-4) + censer transformation (8:5) + 4-element theophany (adds seismos to astrapē/phōnē/brontē); seal→trumpet hinge; libanotos hapax (G3031) - Rev 11:15-19 — Seventh trumpet: kingdom announcement; egeneto (proleptic aorist); ēlthen hē orgē at 11:18 (VP174); five-element programmatic statement at 11:18; 5-element theophany at 11:19; ark revealed (MHP opened); Janus verse; TM071 missing third-woe closure - Rev 16:17-21 — Seventh bowl: gegonen (G1096, perfect active indicative) — consummation marker; 5+ element theophany; Babylon remembered; greatest earthquake; harvest of hail; the terminal bowl climax
