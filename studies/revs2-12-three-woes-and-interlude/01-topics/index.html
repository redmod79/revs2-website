    Topics Research¶

    Question¶

    How do trumpets 5-7 (the three "woes") differ structurally from trumpets 1-4? What is the role of the trumpet interlude (the little book and the two witnesses, Rev 10:1-11:14)? How does the seventh trumpet's kingdom announcement (Rev 11:15-18) function as both climax and transition? What is the relationship between the seventh trumpet and the bowls?

    TRUMPET — SYMBOLICAL¶

    • MAT 24:31
    • 1CO 15:52
    • 1TH 4:16
    • REV 1:10
    • REV 4:1
    • REV 8:1-13
    • REV 9:1-14
    • REV 10:7
    • REV 11:15

    SYMBOLICAL MAT 24:31; 1CO 15:52; 1TH 4:16; REV 1:10; 4:1; 8; 9:1-14; 10:7; 11:15. Made of ram's horn JOS 6:4-6,8,13. Made of silver NUM 10:2. Uses of, prescribed by Moses NUM 10:1-10. Used in war JOB 39:24,25; JER 4:19; 6:1,17; 42:14; 51:27; EZK 7:14; AMO 2:2; 3:6; ZEP 1:16; 1CO 14:8. Used at Mount Sinai EXO 19:13-19; 20:18; HEB 12:19. On the great day of atonement ISA 27:13. At the jubilee LEV 25:9. FIGURATIVE ISA 27:13; EZK 33:3; JOL 2:1; ZEC 9:14; MAT 6:2.

    LOCUST — SYMBOLICAL¶

    • REV 9:3-10
    • JOL 1:4-7
    • JOL 2:2,10
    • EXO 10:1-19

    SYMBOLICAL REV 9:3-10. Authorized as food LEV 11:22. Used as food MAT 3:4; MRK 1:6. Plague of EXO 10:1-19; PSA 105:34,35. Devastation by DEU 28:38; 1KI 8:37; 2CH 7:13; ISA 33:4; JOL 1:4-7; REV 9:7-10. Sun obscured by JOL 2:2,10. Instincts of PRO 30:27. FIGURATIVE JER 46:23.

    PLAGUE — FORETOLD¶

    • REV 11:6
    • REV 15:1
    • REV 15:6-8
    • REV 16:1-21
    • REV 22:18-19

    Foretold REV 11:6; 15:1,6-8; 16; 22:18,19. As a judgment on the Egyptians PSA 105; 135:8,9; ACT 7:36. The plague of Blood EXO 7:14-25; Frogs EXO 8:1-15; Lice EXO 8:16-19; Flies EXO 8:20; On cattle EXO 9:1-7; Of boils and open sores EXO 9:8-12; Hail EXO 9:18-34; Locusts EXO 10:1-20; Darkness EXO 10:21-23; Death of the firstborn EXO 11:4-7; 12:17,29,30. Denounced as a judgment LEV 26:21; DEU 28:59.

    PIT — BOTTOMLESS PIT¶

    • REV 9:1
    • REV 9:2
    • REV 9:11
    • REV 11:7
    • REV 17:8
    • REV 20:1
    • REV 20:3

    The bottomless pit REV 9:1,2,11; 11:7; 17:8; 20:1,3. FIGURATIVE PSA 7:15,16; 40:2; 57:6; 69:15; 119:85; PRO 23:27; 26:27; 28:10; ECC 10:8; JER 48:44.

    WITNESS — FIGURATIVE¶

    • REV 11:3
    • DEU 17:6
    • DEU 19:15
    • MAT 18:16
    • JHN 8:17
    • 2CO 13:1

    Of instruction in righteousness REV 11:3. Two necessary to establish a fact NUM 35:30; DEU 17:6; 19:15; MAT 18:16; JHN 8:17; 2CO 13:1; 1TI 5:19; HEB 10:28. Required to throw the first stone in executing the sentence DEU 13:9; 17:5-7; ACT 7:58.

    KINGDOM OF HEAVEN — GENERAL¶

    • MAT 13:24-30
    • MAT 13:31-32
    • MAT 13:33
    • MAT 13:44
    • MAT 13:45
    • MAT 13:47-50
    • MAT 18:23-35
    • MAT 25:1-13
    • JHN 18:36
    • ROM 14:17

    Compared to a man who sowed good seed MAT 13:24-30,38-43; MRK 4:26-29. To a granule of mustard seed MAT 13:31,32; MRK 4:30,31; LUK 13:18,19. To leaven (yeast) MAT 13:33; LUK 13:21. To a treasure MAT 13:44. To a pearl MAT 13:45. To a net MAT 13:47-50. My kingdom is not of this world JHN 18:36. Does not consist of meat and drink ROM 14:17.

    EARTHQUAKES — SYMBOLICAL¶

    • REV 6:12-14
    • REV 11:13
    • REV 16:18,20

    SYMBOLICAL REV 6:12-14; 11:13; 16:18,20. Prophecies of EZK 38:19; ZEC 14:4; MAT 24:7; MRK 13:8; LUK 21:11; REV 11:19. INSTANCES OF At Sinai EXO 19:18; PSA 68:8; 77:18; 114:4-7; HEB 12:26.

    LIGHTNING — GENERAL¶

    • REV 4:5
    • REV 8:5
    • REV 11:19
    • REV 16:18
    • EZK 1:13-14
    • DAN 10:6

    General scriptures concerning JOB 28:26; 37:3; 38:25,35; PSA 18:14; 77:18; 78:48; 97:4; 135:7; 144:6; JER 10:13; 51:16; EZK 1:13,14; DAN 10:6; NAM 2:4; ZEC 9:14; 10:1; MAT 24:27; 28:3; LUK 10:18; REV 4:5; 8:5; 11:19; 16:18.

    SANCTUARY — DIVINE DWELLING¶

    • EXO 25:8
    • HEB 8:2,5
    • HEB 9:2
    • REV 11:19
    • REV 15:5

    (The Holy of Holies) In the tabernacle HEB 9:2. Divine dwelling place EXO 25:8. Reverence for LEV 19:30; 26:2. Symbolical HEB 8:2,5. See TABERNACLE. See TEMPLE.

    COVENANT — GENERAL¶

    • EXO 24:7-8
    • JER 34:8-22
    • GAL 3:15

    Sacred JOS 9:18-21; GAL 3:15. Binding JOS 9:18-20; JER 34:8-21; EZK 17:14-18; GAL 3:15. Blood of EXO 24:8. Book of EXO 24:7. The Mosaic law called a covenant EXO 34:28.
