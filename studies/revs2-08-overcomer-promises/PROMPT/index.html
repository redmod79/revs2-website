    Question¶

    What are the seven overcomer promises (Rev 2:7, 2:11, 2:17, 2:26-28, 3:5, 3:12, 3:21), and how do they escalate across the letter sequence? Do they form a progression from individual blessing (tree of life) to cosmic enthronement (sitting on Christ's throne)? How does each promise find its fulfillment in Revelation 21-22's New Jerusalem vision? Does the overcomer arc create a structural bridge from the letters to the book's conclusion?

    Prior Research Summary¶

    Five prior studies provide substantial background. revs-12-overcomer-promises established SP029 (Strong: all 7 promises have VP to Rev 19-22, 7/7 mapping) and SP030 (Moderate: promises escalate from individual sustenance to cosmic co-regency, but measurable escalation not demonstrated at every step). revs2-06 documented the eight-element template (SP021 Strong) and ear/overcomer reversal (SP022 Strong). revs2-07 established the letter chiasm at Moderate (B-B' Strong only). The nikao-overcoming-chain study mapped 17 Revelation occurrences with tense distribution. rev-22-new-jerusalem-tabernacles documented the naos progression and Genesis-Revelation bookend (SP111). Web research surfaced Minear's promise-to-fulfillment mapping (via Storms), Kline's antithetical parallelism thesis, Leithart's creation-day mapping, Bauckham's christological redefinition of nikao, and Benware's two-group reading of 21:7-8.

    Capstone Promise and Antithesis: Rev 21:7-8 as culminating overcomer statement - Rev 21:7 — Capstone overcomer promise: inherit all things, sonship - Rev 21:8 — Antithetical contrast: second death for non-overcomers — two-group structure

    Promise 1 Fulfillment (Tree of Life): Tree of life in New Jerusalem - Rev 22:2 — Fulfillment of Promise 1: tree of life in New Jerusalem - Rev 22:14 — Fulfillment of Promise 1: right to tree of life - Gen 2:9 — OT source: tree of life in the garden — Eden origin of Promise 1 - Gen 3:22 — OT source: barred from tree of life — what overcomer promise reverses - Gen 3:24 — OT source: cherubim guard tree of life — Genesis-Revelation inclusio

    Promise 2 Fulfillment (Second Death): Second death resolved - Rev 20:6 — Fulfillment of Promise 2: second death has no power on first-resurrection saints - Rev 20:14 — Fulfillment of Promise 2: death and hell cast into lake of fire = second death - Rev 21:4 — No more death — second death resolved - Rev 21:8 — Antithetical contrast: second death for non-overcomers — two-group structure

    Promise 3/5/6 Fulfillment (Names): Name thread across fulfillments - Rev 19:12 — Fulfillment of Promise 3 parallel: name written no man knew but himself - Rev 22:4 — Fulfillment of Promises 3/5/6: his name in their foreheads - Rev 21:27 — Fulfillment of Promise 5: written in Lamb's book of life

    Promise 4 Fulfillment (Rod of Iron / Morning Star): Authority and morning star - Rev 19:15 — Fulfillment of Promise 4: rule nations with rod of iron - Rev 22:16 — Fulfillment of Promise 4: Christ IS the bright and morning star - Ps 2:8-9 — OT source: rod of iron, rule nations — source for Promise 4

    Promise 5 Fulfillment (White Raiment / Book of Life): Garment and book imagery - Rev 19:8 — Fulfillment of Promise 5: fine linen, clean and white = righteousness of saints - Rev 19:14 — Fulfillment of Promise 5: armies in white linen - Rev 20:12 — Book of life at judgment — structural link to Promise 5 - Rev 21:27 — Fulfillment of Promise 5: written in Lamb's book of life

    Promise 6 Fulfillment (Temple / New Jerusalem): Naos tension and city descent - Rev 21:2 — Fulfillment of Promise 6: New Jerusalem descending from heaven - Rev 21:22 — Fulfillment/tension with Promise 6: no temple therein — naos tension

    Promise 7 Fulfillment (Throne): Throne-sharing and reign - Rev 20:4 — Fulfillment of Promise 7: thrones, lived and reigned with Christ - Rev 22:3 — Fulfillment of Promise 7: throne of God and the Lamb, servants serve him - Rev 22:5 — Fulfillment of Promise 7: they shall reign forever and ever

    Nikao Thread Across Revelation: Every nikao occurrence in Revelation - Rev 2:7 — Promise 1 (Ephesus): tree of life in paradise of God - Rev 2:11 — Promise 2 (Smyrna): not hurt of second death - Rev 2:17 — Promise 3 (Pergamos): hidden manna, white stone, new name - Rev 3:5 — Promise 5 (Sardis): white raiment, book of life, name confessed - Rev 3:12 — Promise 6 (Philadelphia): pillar in temple, name of God/city/new name - Rev 3:21 — Promise 7 (Laodicea): sit with Christ on his throne - Rev 5:5 — Lion of Judah has prevailed (enikesen) — Christ as paradigmatic overcomer - Rev 12:11 — Saints overcame (enikesen) by blood of Lamb — means of overcoming - Rev 15:2 — Those who had gotten the victory (nikao) over the beast - Rev 17:14 — Lamb SHALL overcome them — future resolution of nikao arc - Rev 21:7 — Capstone overcomer promise: inherit all things, sonship
