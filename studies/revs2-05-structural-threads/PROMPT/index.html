    Bible Study: What recurring vocabulary threads run through the entire book of Revelation — "faithful and true" (pistos kai alethinos), "kings and priests" (basileis kai hiereis), the overcomer (nikao) arc, "testimony of Jesus" (he martyria Iesou), "word of God" (ho logos tou theou), "Almighty" (pantokrator) — and how do they create structural cohesion across the four visions?¶

    Question¶

    What recurring vocabulary threads run through the entire book of Revelation — "faithful and true" (pistos kai alethinos), "kings and priests" (basileis kai hiereis), the overcomer (nikao) arc, "testimony of Jesus" (he martyria Iesou), "word of God" (ho logos tou theou), "Almighty" (pantokrator) — and how do they create structural cohesion across the four visions?

    Prior Research Summary¶

    revs-07 studied three threads (pistos/alethinos, basileian/hiereis, nikao) and found VP031-VP037, SP014-SP017, AN008. This study expands to six threads (adding testimony of Jesus, word of God, pantokrator) and maps ALL six against the four-vision framework from revs2-01. The revs2-01 study established en pneumati boundaries: Vision 1 (1:9-3:22), Vision 2 (4:1-16:21), Vision 3 (17:1-21:8), Vision 4 (21:9-22:5). revs2-02 confirmed the prologue-epilogue inclusio with 9+ verbal parallels. nikao-overcoming-chain found 17 Revelation occurrences with structured tense distribution. revs-12 mapped overcomer promises to fulfillment passages. Web research found 13 scholarly sources confirming Bauckham's 'stock phrases with minor variations' framework and the Leitwort/Leitwortstil technique.
