    Verses¶

    Question¶

    How do Revelation's symbolic numbers form a coherent system? How do 7 (divine completeness), 12 (covenant people), 4 (creation/universality), 3.5 (broken completeness), 1000 (consummation fullness), 666 (counterfeit parody), 24 (elders), 3, 10, and 144,000 relate to each other mathematically and theologically? How does the fraction escalation (1/4 → 1/3 → total) fit the system?

    Rev 1:4¶

    Notes: Seven churches + seven Spirits: hepta establishes the divine organizing principle from the book's first verse. The temporal formula (is/was/is-to-come) is in its full three-element form here. Seven Spirits = Holy Spirit in sevenfold presentation (revs2-14).

    Rev 1:8¶

    Notes: Alpha/Omega (stage 1 of 3). Full three-element temporal formula. Pantokrator (G3841) here at V1 boundary — only thread spanning all four en pneumati visions (revs2-26). Seven = divine completeness framing the book.

    Rev 1:20¶

    Notes: Four occurrences of hepta in one verse (stars, golden candlesticks, stars again, churches). The book decodes its own symbols here: 7 stars = 7 church angels; 7 candlesticks = 7 churches. This is the first explicit seven-structure decoding in Revelation.

    Rev 2:10¶

    Notes: Ten days of tribulation at Smyrna. Deka (G1176) here marks a bounded, specific period of trial. This is the only Revelation occurrence of deka for a time period (as opposed to ten horns/ten kings). Context: concrete limited duration, not symbolic infinity.

    Rev 2:7¶

    Notes: First of seven overcomer promises across seven letters (Rev 2:7, 2:11, 2:17, 2:26; 3:5, 3:12, 3:21). Each letter ends with an overcomer promise — the seven-letter structure generates a heptad of promises fulfilled in the New Jerusalem (revs2-26).

    Rev 4:4¶

    Notes: Twenty-four (24 = 12+12) elders at the throne. Eikositessares = 20 + 4 compound. First appearance of this number. The 24 priestly courses of 1 Chr 24:1-4 (16 sons of Eleazar + 8 sons of Ithamar = 24) provide OT background. Alternatively 12 tribes + 12 apostles = 24. Presbyteros (G4245) appears exactly 12 times in Revelation, all for the 24 elders.

    Rev 4:5¶

    Notes: Seven lamps = seven Spirits of God. This identifies the seven Spirits with the seven-branched menorah (Exod 25:37; Zech 4:2). Theophany formula baseline: 3 elements (lightnings, thunderings, voices) — TM081 station 1 (revs2-26).

    Rev 4:6¶

    Notes: Four living creatures (tessares zoa) — first appearance. These four guard the throne and drive the entire seal/trumpet/bowl sequences structurally. Tessares (G4245) = 42 NT occ, 30 (71%) in Revelation. The four creatures distribute seals 1-4, the four winds (7:1-3), and the four bowls.

    Rev 4:8¶

    Notes: Four creatures, six wings each, triple holy. Full three-element divine temporal formula (was/is/is-to-come). Pantokrator at V2 throne-room (one of 9 Rev occurrences). The triple 'holy' parallels triple structure of 666 as an inversion — divine triple completeness vs. beast triple falling-short.

    Rev 4:10¶

    Notes: Twenty-four elders worship. The 24 elders and 4 creatures appear together 7 times in Revelation (revs2-17). Note: 24 = 2 × 12, maintaining the 12-system arithmetic.

    Rev 5:5¶

    Notes: Seven seals = the seven-sealed scroll. The Lamb is the only one worthy to open it (5:9). The seven seals initiate the first judgment heptad. Note: Lion expected, Lamb seen (5:6) — the hear/see pattern (same paradigm as 144,000 in 7:4 → 7:9).

    Rev 5:6¶

    Notes: Seven horns + seven eyes = seven Spirits sent into all the earth. The Lamb has seven horns (fullness of power) and seven eyes (fullness of omniscience). The number 7 is embedded in the Lamb's very appearance. Arnion (G721): 29 of 30 NT occurrences are Christological (revs2-19).

    Rev 5:8¶

    Notes: Four creatures + 24 elders together at the Lamb's investiture. The 4+24 pairing appears 7 times in Revelation. Phiale (golden vials) = vessel arc element (revs2-26); 24 elders each hold a harp and a vial.

    Rev 5:9¶

    Notes: First of seven confirmed fourfold ethnic formula occurrences (Rev 5:9; 7:9; 10:11; 11:9; 13:7; 14:6; 17:15 — revs2-17). Tessares not grammatically present here but the fourfold pattern is: kindred, tongue, people, nation. The 4-element universal coverage formula appears 7 times (4×7 = structural completeness).

    Rev 5:12¶

    Notes: Sevenfold doxology: power, riches, wisdom, strength, honour, glory, blessing — seven attributes ascribed to the Lamb. This is the first of several heptadic doxologies in Revelation. Contrast with 666: the Lamb receives seven (fullness) while the beast's number is triple-six (parody of seven).

    Rev 6:1¶

    Notes: Four living creatures summon the four horsemen (seals 1-4). The first structural function of tessares: distributing the four seals of judgment. Each of the four creatures summons one horseman.

    Rev 6:8¶

    Notes: Tetarton = one quarter (1/4). The seal judgment scope. This is Stage 1 of the fraction escalation system (SP036): 1/4 → 1/3 → total. G5067 tetartos (ordinal 'fourth') here. The four-fold kills (sword, hunger, death, beasts) mirror the four-fold ethnic formula structurally.

    Rev 7:4¶

    Notes: 144,000 = 12 × 12 × 1,000 = 12² × 10³. The number uses: arithmon (G706, number), hekatón (G1540, hundred), tessarakonta (G5062, forty), tessares (G5064, four), chiliades (G5505, thousand-groups). Greek parsing confirms: arithmon ton esphragismenon, hekaton tessarakonta tessares chiliades. John hears 144,000 (7:4), then sees innumerable multitude (7:9) — same hear/see paradigm as Rev 5:5-6.

    Rev 7:5-8¶

    Notes: Twelve tribes × twelve thousand each = 144,000. Anomalies (revs2-17): Judah listed first (not Reuben), Dan absent, Levi included (normally excluded from land-based lists), Manasseh alongside Joseph (his son). 12 × 12,000 = 144,000. Each tribe assignment: dodeka chiliades (twelve thousands). The mathematical logic: 12 = covenant people, 1,000 = fullness/consummation.

    Rev 7:9¶

    Notes: The innumerable multitude: arithmon (G706, number) — 'no man could number' (arith-meo used negatively). Second of seven fourfold ethnic formulas: nations, kindreds, people, tongues. The 144,000 (numbered, specific) and innumerable multitude are not two groups but two perspectives on the same group (hear/see paradigm).

    Rev 8:7¶

    Notes: To triton (the third part) — first appearance in trumpet sequence. G5154 tritos (ordinal 'third') begins the 1/3 pattern. Stage 2 of fraction escalation (SP036): 1/3 scope for trumpets. TM075: 'to triton' appears 11 times in Rev 8-9 for the trumpet sequence (confirmed per PROMPT.md verification directive). Zero in Rev 16 (bowl sequence is total scope).

    Rev 8:13¶

    Notes: Triple woe (treis ouai) marks the 4+3 subdivision of the seven trumpets. This is the structural divider: trumpets 1-4 (cosmic/ecology targets) vs. trumpets 5-7 (humanity/woe targets). Treis (G5140) = three remaining trumpet angels. The triple ouai mirrors the triple 'holy' of Rev 4:8 structurally — both are triadic completeness markers.

    Rev 9:15¶

    Notes: Four angels at the Euphrates (fourfold creation-universality pattern). The third part (to triton) of men slain. Tessares angels at geo-strategic position. The four-fold time specification (hour, day, month, year) reinforces the creation/totality dimension of four.

    Rev 9:18¶

    Notes: Treis (G5140) = three plague agents (fire, smoke, brimstone). To triton = the third part of men killed. This verse pairs three (triad of judgment agents) with the third (fraction of destruction). Note: the three agents come from Rev 9:17 description — the plagues are a trinity of destruction.

    Rev 10:3¶

    Notes: Seven thunders — the only hidden heptad (sealed by divine command in 10:4). The seven thunders are the exception that proves the rule: all other heptads are revealed and disclosed; this one is sealed. This is structurally significant as a demonstration that seven structures the unseen as well as the seen.

    Rev 11:2¶

    Notes: Forty-two months (42 months = 3.5 years). The outer court given to Gentiles. SP079 Strong: 42 months ALWAYS with hostile subjects (revs2-18). Tessarakonta (G5062) + duo (G1417) = compound 42. The 3.5-year hostile period marks broken divine completeness: 7 ÷ 2 = 3.5, i.e., divine completeness interrupted at midpoint.

    Rev 11:3¶

    Notes: 1,260 days (= 42 months = 3.5 years, alternative expression). SP079 Strong: 1260 days ALWAYS with preservation subjects (revs2-18). Two witnesses (duo = G1417) prophesy during the preservation period. The number two here: minimum witness requirement (Deut 17:6 / 19:15; 2 Cor 13:1).

    Rev 11:9¶

    Notes: Three and a half days (treis hēmeras kai hēmisy = 3.5 days). Third of seven fourfold ethnic formulas: people, kindreds, tongues, nations. The 3.5-day period mirrors 3.5 years — the fraction of broken completeness applied at a smaller scale.

    Rev 11:11¶

    Notes: After three and a half days (meta tas treis hēmeras kai hēmisy). The Spirit of life (pneuma G4151) enters — resurrection parallel. The 3.5-day period of apparent defeat is reversed, mirroring the pattern of 3.5-year hostility followed by divine intervention.

    Rev 11:13¶

    Notes: Tenth part (G1181 dekatos = ordinal tenth) of the city fell. Seven thousand slain. Both deka and hepta in the same verse. The tenth fraction here is not part of the 1/4 → 1/3 → total escalation sequence but a localized judgment proportion. Seven thousand = a specific but complete number of judgment.

    Rev 11:15¶

    Notes: Seventh (hebdomos G1442) trumpet. The climactic conclusion of the second heptad. Ginomai arc Stage 1: egeneto (proleptic aorist, V-2ADI-3S) announces kingdom accomplished. TM071: woe 3 never formally closed — 7th trumpet contains the bowls within itself (telescoping). Pantokrator at 11:17.

    Rev 12:1¶

    Notes: Crown of twelve stars — dodeka (G1427) = covenant people. The twelve stars mirror the 12 tribes/12 apostles. The woman's crown marks her as the covenant community. Dodeka: 22 Revelation occurrences, exclusively positive — zero demonic uses (revs2-17).

    Rev 12:3¶

    Notes: Dragon: 7 heads, 10 horns, 7 crowns (diademata). Diadema arc stage 1: dragon 7 diadems (revs2-19). Seven heads = the seven-king structure (Rev 17:9-10). Ten horns = ten kings of political power (Rev 17:12). Seven + ten on the dragon's body — both numbers are in the parody system but placed on a beast (Rev 13:1: 10 crowns, not 7).

    Rev 12:6¶

    Notes: 1,260 days (chilia = G5507 + 200 + 60). Another expression of 3.5 years in preservation mode. This is the same period as Rev 11:3 but with the woman (covenant community) as subject. SP079 Strong confirmed: 1260 days = preservation, not hostile period.

    Rev 12:14¶

    Notes: Time, times, half a time (kairos + kairoi + hemisys kairou = 1 + 2 + 0.5 = 3.5). Greek parsing: kairon kai kairous kai hēmisy kairou — virtually quotes LXX Dan 7:25 (revs2-18). The three forms of 3.5-year expression: 42 months (hostile), 1260 days (preservation), time+times+half (prophetic). This is the third form.

    Rev 13:1¶

    Notes: Beast: 7 heads, 10 horns, 10 crowns. Diadema arc stage 2: beast has 10 diadems (vs. dragon's 7). Progression: 7 → 10 → many (Christ, Rev 19:12). Ten crowns on the horns = political power displayed on the military symbols. The beast's claim escalates the dragon's by 3 diadems.

    Rev 13:5¶

    Notes: 42 months — second occurrence of the hostile 42-month period. SP079: 42 months ALWAYS with hostile subjects. The beast's activity window is a divine passive (edothē) — God permits the 42-month hostile period. The beast's authority to operate is bounded and bracketed.

    Rev 13:11¶

    Notes: Two horns like a lamb (arnion G721). The false prophet uses the Lamb's specific word — arnion (G721), not amnos. Duo horns mimic Christ's lamb-like appearance. The sharpest parody of the arnion title: sole non-Christological use of arnion (revs2-19). Two = minimum witness count / judicial threshold, here inverted as two horns of deception.

    Rev 13:18¶

    Notes: The cipher 666 (chi-xi-stigma G5516, or spelled out: hexakosioi hexekonta hex). Greek parsing: G706 arithmos used 3x in Rev 13:17-18 (rhetorical emphasis). The cipher = 600 + 60 + 6 = three iterations of 6 (one short of 7).
