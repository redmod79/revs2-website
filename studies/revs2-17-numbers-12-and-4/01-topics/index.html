    Topics Research¶

    Question¶

    How do the numbers 12 (and its multiples: 24 elders, 144 cubits, 12,000 stadia, 144,000 sealed) and 4 (living creatures, horsemen, angels at corners, winds, universal formulas like 'every kindred, tongue, people, and nation') function as structural and symbolic numbers in Revelation? Document every occurrence of each number. Does 12 consistently represent God's people and 4 represent creation/universality? How do these secondary numbers relate to the primary structural number 7?

    APOSTLES — GENERAL¶

    • Luk 6:13
    • Mat 10:2-4
    • Mar 3:16-19
    • Luk 6:13-16
    • Act 1:13
    • Mat 4:18-22
    • Mat 10:1
    • Mat 28:19-20
    • Mar 3:14-15
    • Luk 22:28-30
    • Act 4:13
    • Mat 16:19
    • Mat 18:18
    • Mat 19:28

    A title distinguishing the twelve disciples, whom Jesus selected to be intimately associated with himself LUK 6:13. Names of MAT 10:2-4; MRK 3:16-19; LUK 6:13-16; ACT 1:13,26. Selection of MAT 4:18-22; 9:9,10; 10:2-4; MRK 3:13-19; LUK 6:13-16; JHN 1:43. Commission of MAT 10; 28:19,20; MRK 3:14,15; 6:7-11; 16:15; LUK 9:1-5; 22:28-30; JHN 20:23; 21:15-19; ACT 1; 2; 10:42. Authority of MAT 16:19; 18:18; 19:28. FALSE 2CO 11:13; REV 2:2.

    • Gen 35:23
    • Gen 34:1-31

    MANASSEH — TRIBE¶

    • Gen 41:50-51
    • Gen 46:20
    • Gen 48:1-20
    • Rev 7:6
    • Num 1:34-35
    • Num 26:29-34
    • Jos 16:9
    • Jos 17:5-11

    Son of Joseph and Asenath GEN 41:50,51; 46:20. Adopted by Jacob on his deathbed GEN 48:1,5-20. Called MANASSES REV 7:6. The two sons of Joseph, Ephraim and Manasseh, were reckoned equally among the primogenitors of the twelve tribes (taking the places of Joseph and Levi). Reallotment of territory to, by Ezekiel EZK 48:4.

    • Psa 9:17

    Sins of ISA 30:1,2. Perish PSA 9:17; ISA 60:12. Righteousness exalts PRO 14:34.
