    03-Analysis — revs2-30: Grand Structural Synthesis¶

    Question¶

    What is the literary architecture and structural pattern system of the book of Revelation?

    Verse-by-Verse Analysis¶

    Rev 1:1-3 — Prologue: SP009 Opens; Ha Dei Genesthai Corner 1¶

    Context: The book opens with a mediation chain: God → Christ → angel → John → servants. "Things which must shortly come to pass" renders ha dei genesthai, the phrase from Dan 2:28 (Aramaic mah di leheweh). This is the first of four structural corners. Direct statement: The book announces itself as an unveiling — Revelation means disclosure, not concealment — and immediately frames its content as prophetic necessity derived from the Daniel revelation. Original language: Ha dei genesthai (what must come to pass) links structurally to Rev 4:1 and Rev 22:6, forming a three-point internal frame.
