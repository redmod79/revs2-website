    Question¶

    What is the structural pattern of trumpets 1-4 — the '1/3 destruction' pattern? Document what is struck, what fraction is affected, and what the result is for each trumpet. How do trumpets 1-4 parallel the Exodus plagues? Use Exodus 7-12 as the source text and document the element-by-element mapping. What structural distinction separates the first four trumpets from the three woes (trumpets 5-7)?

    Prior Research Summary¶

    Seven prior studies provide substantial foundation. Revs-18 documented the formulaic pattern (esalpisen G4537, TM076), the 11 occurrences of to triton (G5154, TM075), verbal parallels VP085-088, fraction escalation SP036, 4+3 division SP038, and creation-domain targeting SP041. Revs2-10 established the sanctuary context (DOA censer parallel AN022, censer reversal, Sinai-theophany escalation TM057, altar vindication arc SP037). Revs2-03 provided the throne room anchor (vessel transformation arc, temple-opening progression). Revs-40 mapped trumpet-plague correspondences comprehensively (SP098-101). The one-third-trumpets study traced the OT background for 1/3 from Ezekiel 5 and Zechariah 13. Web research identified Robinson (2022) on creation-reversal, Mueller (2021) on Rev 14:7/trumpet-target correspondence, Beale on Ezekiel 5 as 1/3 source, Bauckham on 4+1+(1+intercalation)+1 structure, and Wadholm on Joel-Revelation verbal parallels.

    Primary Verses (30 — full analysis required)¶

    Four trumpet texts: The primary passage — each trumpet with its target, fraction, and result - Rev 8:6 — Formulaic introduction — seven angels prepare to sound - Rev 8:7 — First trumpet — hail and fire mingled with blood, 1/3 trees and all green grass burnt - Rev 8:8-9 — Second trumpet — great mountain burning with fire cast into sea, 1/3 sea blood, 1/3 creatures died, 1/3 ships destroyed - Rev 8:10-11 — Third trumpet — great star Wormwood falls on 1/3 rivers and fountains, waters made bitter, many men died - Rev 8:12 — Fourth trumpet — 1/3 sun, moon, stars smitten; rhetorical climax with 5 occurrences of to triton - Rev 8:13 — Structural dividing verse — eagle/angel announces three woes, marking 4+3 division

    Incense prelude: Sanctuary context for the trumpet sequence — DOA censer, prayer-to-judgment transition - Rev 8:2-5 — Incense prelude — sanctuary context establishing trumpet judgments as liturgical sequence

    Exodus plague sources: OT plague passages paralleling each trumpet - Exo 7:17-21 — Plague 1 — waters to blood, fish died, river stank; source for second trumpet parallel (VP086) - Exo 10:21-23 — Plague 9 — three days of darkness; source for fourth trumpet parallel (VP087)

    OT fraction background: Ezekiel and Zechariah third-part passages - Ezek 5:1-4 — Tripartite division of hair — fire, sword, scattering — source for 1/3 fraction concept - Ezek 5:12 — Explicit 'a third part shall die with pestilence' — direct OT background for to triton pattern - Zech 13:8-9 — Inverted 1/3 pattern — 2/3 cut off, surviving 1/3 refined; counterpart to Ezekiel's destructive 1/3

    Prophetic wormwood sources: Jeremiah's wormwood-as-judgment-for-apostasy passages - Jer 9:13-15 — Wormwood (laanah H3939) as judgment for forsaking God's law — source for third trumpet (VP088) - Jer 23:13-15 — Wormwood fed to false prophets — second OT source for third trumpet wormwood imagery

    Day-of-the-LORD darkness: Prophetic celestial darkening tradition - Isa 13:10 — Day of the LORD — sun darkened, moon no light, stars no light; direct parallel to fourth trumpet - Joel 2:1-2 — Trumpet blown in Zion, day of darkness and gloominess — trumpet-warning-darkness triad - Joel 2:10 — Earth quakes, sun and moon dark, stars withdraw shining — parallels fourth trumpet darkening - Joel 2:30-31 — Blood, fire, pillars of smoke; sun turned to darkness, moon to blood — verbal parallels with Rev 8:7-12

    Bowl parallels for escalation: Bowl judgments for comparison with trumpet targets and fractions - Rev 16:2-4 — Bowls 1-3 — same targets (earth, sea, rivers) but total destruction, completing escalation - Rev 16:8-10 — Bowls 4-5 — sun (scorching, not darkening — contrast with trumpet 4) and darkness of beast's seat

    Seal fraction for escalation: 1/4 fraction at seals for escalation comparison - Rev 6:8 — Fourth seal — 1/4 of earth; establishes lower fraction for escalation comparison (SP036)

    Creation domain references: Genesis creation days and Revelation creation formulae - Gen 1:9-12 — Creation Day 3 — earth, seas, vegetation; maps to trumpet targets 1-3 (SP041) - Gen 1:14-18 — Creation Day 4 — sun, moon, stars to rule day/night; maps to trumpet 4 target - Rev 14:7 — Creation formula — heaven, earth, sea, fountains of waters — matches four trumpet ecological targets

    Failed repentance and Amos parallel: Trumpet sequence conclusion and OT escalating-judgment-without-repentance - Rev 9:20-21 — Failed repentance conclusion — survivors 'repented not' despite plagues; structural bookend - Amos 4:6-12 — Five escalating judgments with 'yet have ye not returned unto me' — closest OT structural parallel to trumpet-failed-repentance pattern

    Plague-reordering precedent: OT psalms recapitulating plagues in non-Exodus order - Psa 105:28-36 — Psalm recapitulation of plagues in non-Exodus order — OT precedent for reordering plagues

    Focus Areas¶

    1. The 1/3 fraction pattern — textual markers and OT background: The to triton (G5154) construction appears 11 times in Rev 8:7-12, with 5 occurrences concentrated in 8:12 alone forming the rhetorical climax (previously cataloged as TM075). READ $WORD_STUDIES/G5154-tritos.md for the canonical word study. Run greek_parser.py --verse on each verse from Rev 8:7 through 8:12 to capture the exact Greek construction (to triton + partitive genitive). The research agent should document whether the article + adjective pattern is consistent across all 11 occurrences. For the OT background, retrieve Ezekiel 5:1-12 — this is Beale's primary source for the 1/3 fraction, using H7992 shliyshiy in a tripartite destruction scheme (fire, sword, scattering). Then retrieve Zechariah 13:8-9 for the inverted pattern (2/3 destroyed, 1/3 refined). Run hebrew_parser.py on Ezek 5:2,12 and Zech 13:8-9 to compare the Hebrew construction. The prior study (one-third-trumpets) concluded that Revelation synthesizes both OT traditions, destroying 1/3 while leaving 2/3 as potential remnant. Verify this structural observation.
