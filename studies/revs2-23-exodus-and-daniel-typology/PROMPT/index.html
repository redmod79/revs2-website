    Question¶

    How does Revelation systematically draw on the Exodus plagues for its trumpet and bowl judgments? Map all ten Exodus plagues to their Revelation counterparts — document shared vocabulary, shared imagery, and transformations. Are the bowls closer to the Exodus plagues than the trumpets? How does Daniel 7's beast imagery (lion, bear, leopard, terrible beast) combine into Revelation's composite sea beast (Rev 13:2)? Use Daniel 7:17,23 as the explicit structural decoder. How does Daniel 7:9-10 serve as the template for Rev 20:11-13?

    Prior Research Summary¶

    Six directly relevant prior studies found. revs2-11 established: T1=Plague 7 (hail/fire, VP085, Strong), T2=Plague 1 (blood, VP086, Moderate), T3=no plague (Jeremiah wormwood), T4=Plague 9 (darkness, VP087); 1/3 fraction pattern (SP036) across trumpets from Ezek 5:1-12; Joel as prophetic intermediary for locust/T5 connection; Rev 9:20 applies plegais retroactively to all trumpets. revs2-13 established: SP055 (Strong) — trumpet-bowl domain correspondence 100% zero-rearrangement; SP056 — impenitence escalation; SP119 — phiale bowl transformation; B1=Plague 6 (helkos G1668, PMI 10.64); B7=Plague 7 (chalaza G5464, PMI 29.60); Song of Moses at Rev 15:3 as explicit Exodus bridge; frog-spirits (batrachos G944, NT hapax) at Rev 16:13. revs-40 established the comprehensive 10-plague table (SP099 Moderate: bowls closer, 6/7 vs 5/7). revs-41 established: TM204 (reversed beast order), TM205 (stoma laloun megala verbatim Dan 7:8/20 LXX = Rev 13:5), TM206 (7 heads cumulative from 4 Daniel beasts), TM207 (Rev 1:14 merges Ancient of Days + Son of Man); SP108 (Strong: composite absorption); VP12/AN9 (Strong: Rev 20:11-12 = Dan 7:9-10); VP22 (Strong: Rev 20:4 = Dan 7:22). dan-7-judgment-antitypical-doa established: Dan 7 = forensic process (books examined); Malachi 3:16 faithfulness records; books not limited to sin-records.
