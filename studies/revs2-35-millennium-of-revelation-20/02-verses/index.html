      • Survivors keeping feast of tabernacles — postmillennial proof text

    Verses¶

    Question¶

    What does Revelation 20 say about the millennium — the binding of Satan, the first resurrection, the reign of saints, the release of Gog and Magog, and the great white throne judgment — and what do the text’s own structural, grammatical, and vocabulary indicators establish about the nature, sequence, and theological function of these events within Revelation’s macro-structure?

    FA01: Satan Bound in the Abyss¶

    And I saw an angel come down from heaven, having the key of the bottomless pit and a great chain in his hand.

    Context: Opens millennium narrative with kai eidon transition

    Notes: kai eidon vision formula (SP006). Angel equipped with key (kleis G2807) and chain (halysis G254).

    FA01: Satan Bound in the Abyss¶

    And he laid hold on the dragon, that old serpent, which is the Devil, and Satan, and bound him a thousand years,

    Context: Fourfold Satan ID matching 12:9; krateo + deo verbs; chilia ete first

    Notes: ekratesen V-AAI-3S + edesen V-AAI-3S. Fourfold ID: drakon, ophis archaios, Diabolos, Satanas (VP163).

    FA01: Satan Bound in the Abyss¶

    And cast him into the bottomless pit, and shut him up, and set a seal upon him, that he should deceive the nations no more, till the thousand years should be fulfilled: and after that he must be loosed a little season.

    Context: Triple confinement; purpose clause; mikron chronon release

    Notes: Five AAI verbs: ekratesen, edesen, ebalen, ekleisen, esphragisen (TM178). hina me planese V-AAS-3S. dei lythenai = divine necessity.

    FA08: Ballo Arc¶

    And the great dragon was cast out, that old serpent, called the Devil, and Satan, which deceiveth the whole world: he was cast out into the earth, and his angels were cast out with him.

    Context: First ballo arc station; fourfold ID matching 20:2

    Notes: eblethe V-API-3S passive. ho planon present active = currently deceiving. VP163.

    FA01: Satan Bound in the Abyss¶

    Or else how can one enter into a strong man's house, and spoil his goods, except he first bind the strong man? and then he will spoil his house.

    Context: Amillennial proof text for present binding; compare with Rev 20 five-verb sequence

    Notes: dese V-AAS-3S aorist subjunctive. Burglar analogy vs five-verb confinement.

    FA01: Satan Bound in the Abyss¶

    And it shall come to pass in that day, [that] the LORD shall punish the host of the high ones [that are] on high, and the kings of the earth upon the earth.

    Context: OT millennium template: two-stage punishment of heavenly/earthly powers

    Notes: yifqod H6485 paqad Qal Impf 3ms. tseva hamarom = host of the height.

    FA01: Satan Bound in the Abyss¶

    And they shall be gathered together, [as] prisoners are gathered in the pit, and shall be shut up in the prison, and after many days shall they be visited.

    Context: Two-stage: imprisonment in pit/prison then later visitation

    Notes: bor H953 = pit. misgar H4525 = dungeon. merov yamim yippaqedu = after many days visited. Two-stage matches Rev 20.

    FA11: Premillennial vs Amillennial vs Postmillennial¶

    In whom the god of this world hath blinded the minds of them which believe not, lest the light of the glorious gospel of Christ, who is the image of God, should shine unto them.

    Context: Satan as god of this world; present activity incompatible with Rev 20 binding

    Notes: ho theos tou aionos toutou. Present active deception.

    FA11: Premillennial vs Amillennial vs Postmillennial¶

    Be sober, be vigilant; because your adversary the devil, as a roaring lion, walketh about, seeking whom he may devour:

    Context: Devil as roaring lion; present activity contradicting bound-now reading

    Notes: peripatei V-PAI-3S present active. Contradicts five-verb imprisonment.

    FA11: Premillennial vs Amillennial vs Postmillennial¶

    Wherein in time past ye walked according to the course of this world, according to the prince of the power of the air, the spirit that now worketh in the children of disobedience:

    Context: Prince of the air currently working; Satan active post-cross

    Notes: tou pneumatos tou nyn energountos = the spirit NOW working.

    FA01: Satan Bound in the Abyss¶

    And they besought him that he would not command them to go out into the deep.

    Context: Demons fear the abyss as their prison

    Notes: eis ten abysson G12. Dreaded demonic prison.

    FA01: Satan Bound in the Abyss¶

    And the angels which kept not their first estate, but left their own habitation, he hath reserved in everlasting chains under darkness unto the judgment of the great day.

    Context: Angels reserved in chains for judgment; parallel to Satan binding

    Notes: desmois aidiois hypo zophon = everlasting chains under darkness.

    FA01: Satan Bound in the Abyss¶

    For if God spared not the angels that sinned, but cast [them] down to hell, and delivered [them] into chains of darkness, to be reserved unto judgment;

    Context: Angels cast to Tartarus in chains; confinement parallels Rev 20

    Notes: tartarosas V-AAP-NSM hapax. seirais zophou = chains of darkness.

    FA02: Thrones, Judgment, and Who Sits¶

    And I saw thrones, and they sat upon them, and judgment was given unto them: and [I saw] the souls of them that were beheaded for the witness of Jesus, and for the word of God, and which had not worshipped the beast, neither his image, neither had received [his] mark upon their foreheads, or in their hands; and they lived and reigned with Christ a thousand years.

    Context: Thrones, krima edothe divine passive, martyrs, ezesan, ebasileusan

    Notes: ekathisan V-AAI-3P no explicit subject. krima edothe divine passive paralleling Dan 7:22. ezesan V-AAI-3P ingressive. ebasileusan V-AAI-3P inception.

    FA06: Great White Throne Judgment¶

    I beheld till the thrones were cast down, and the Ancient of days did sit, whose garment [was] white as snow, and the hair of his head like the pure wool: his throne [was like] the fiery flame, [and] his wheels [as] burning fire.

    Context: Thrones set; distributed template at Rev 20:11-12

    Notes: korsawan remiv. atiq yomin yetiv. VP165 distributed template.

    FA06: Great White Throne Judgment¶

    A fiery stream issued and came forth from before him: thousand thousands ministered unto him, and ten thousand times ten thousand stood before him: the judgment was set, and the books were opened.

    Context: Books opened; paralleled at Rev 20:12

    Notes: sifrin petichuw = biblia enoichthesan.

    FA02: Thrones, Judgment, and Who Sits¶

    Until the Ancient of days came, and judgment was given to the saints of the most High; and the time came that the saints possessed the kingdom.

    Context: Direct parallel to krima edothe in Rev 20:4

    Notes: dina yhib leqaddishe elyonin. Passive-grant formula.

    FA02: Thrones, Judgment, and Who Sits¶

    Do ye not know that the saints shall judge the world? and if the world shall be judged by you, are ye unworthy to judge the smallest matters?

    Context: Saints shall judge the world; millennial reign content

    Notes: hoi hagioi ton kosmon krinousin. Future active.

    FA02: Thrones, Judgment, and Who Sits¶

    Know ye not that we shall judge angels? how much more things that pertain to this life?

    Context: Saints shall judge angels; extends judicial authority
