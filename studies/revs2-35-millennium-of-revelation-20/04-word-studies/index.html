    Word Studies¶

    Question¶

    What does Revelation 20 say about the millennium — the binding of Satan, the first resurrection, the reign of saints, the release of Gog and Magog, and the great white throne judgment — and what do the text’s own structural, grammatical, and vocabulary indicators establish about the nature, sequence, and theological function of these events within Revelation’s macro-structure?

    G906 — ballo¶

    Source: G906-ballo.md

    Occurrences: 127

    Cast/throw, 106v/127tok. 23 Rev occurrences trace Satan progressive defeat: heaven->earth->abyss->lake of fire.

    Semantic categories: Throwing, Placing, Casting into judgment, Apocalyptic casting

    Key verses: Rev 12:9, Rev 20:3, Rev 20:10, Rev 20:14

    Relevance: Central ballo arc verb. 13 Rev occurrences = directional chronological trajectory.

    G2198 — zao¶

    Source: G2198-zao.md

    Occurrences: 147

    Live, 127v/147tok. Aorist ezesan (V-AAI-3P) identical at Rev 2:8, 13:14, 20:4, 20:5. All four = bodily coming-to-life.

    Semantic categories: Physical life, Living God, Spiritual life, Ethical conduct, Resurrection life

    Key verses: Rev 20:4, Rev 20:5, Rev 2:8, Rev 13:14

    Relevance: ezesan consistency SP082. Amillennial requires unprecedented spiritual meaning.

    G386 — anastasis¶

    Source: G386-anastasis.md

    Occurrences: 42

    Resurrection, 42 occurrences. 41 = bodily. Only Rev occurrences at 20:5-6. NT has synegeiro for spiritual co-resurrection.

    Semantic categories: Physical resurrection, Disputed doctrine, Apostolic proclamation, Figurative rising

    Key verses: Rev 20:5, Rev 20:6, John 5:29, 1 Cor 15:42

    Relevance: TM176. 41/42 = bodily. Distinct vocabulary exists for spiritual resurrection.

    G4105 — planao¶

    Source: G4105-planao.md

    Occurrences: 51

    Deceive/mislead, 51 occ. Tense chain: 12:9 present active, 20:3 aorist subjunctive neg, 20:8 infinitive, 20:10 present participle.

    Semantic categories: Straying sheep, Intellectual error, End-time deception, Supernatural deception, Self-deception

    Key verses: Rev 12:9, Rev 20:3, Rev 20:8, Rev 20:10

    Relevance: Planao tense chain = strongest internal evidence binding is future from John perspective.

    G12 — abyssos¶

    Source: G12-abyssos.md

    Occurrences: 9

    Abyss, 9 NT occ in 3 books. LXX bridge to tehom (30x, PMI 9.16). Locked demonic prison.

    Semantic categories: Deep as realm, Bottomless pit, Source of beastly ascent, Place of confinement

    Key verses: Rev 20:1, Rev 20:3, Rev 9:1, Luke 8:31

    Relevance: Central to location question. LXX tehom bridge connects to Gen 1:2 desolation.

    G1210 — deo¶

    Source: G1210-deo.md

    Occurrences: 49

    Bind, 41v/49tok. edesen at 20:2. deo/lyo antonym pair frames millennium TM175.

    Semantic categories: Physical binding, Tied objects, Binding/loosing formulae, Legal obligation, Captivity

    Key verses: Rev 20:2, Matt 12:29, Matt 16:19

    Relevance: TM175: deo/lyo frames millennium. edesen opens, lythesetai closes.

    G3089 — lyo¶

    Source: G3089-lyo.md

    Occurrences: 46

    Loose/release, 40v/46tok. Completes deo/lyo at 20:3,7. Also cosmic dissolution 2 Pet 3:10-12.

    Semantic categories: Untying bonds, Binding/loosing authority, Release from captivity, Breaking, Dissolving

    Key verses: Rev 20:3, Rev 20:7, Rev 9:14, 1 John 3:8

    Relevance: lythesetai V-FPI-3S at 20:7 = divine passive loosing.

    G2917 — krima¶

    Source: G2917-krima.md

    Occurrences: 27

    Judgment/verdict, 27 NT occ. krima edothe at 20:4 = divine passive paralleling Dan 7:22. Judgment TO saints.

    Semantic categories: Reciprocal standard, God verdicts, Condemnatory sentence, Vindication of saints, Eschatological judgment

    Key verses: Rev 20:4, Rev 18:20, Rev 17:1, Dan 7:22

    Relevance: krima edothe divine passive. Three-krima arc: 17:1, 18:20, 20:4.

    G936 — basileuo¶

    Source: G936-basileuo.md

    Occurrences: 23

    Reign, 23 occ. Tense arc: ebasileusan aorist 20:4, basileusousin future 20:6, basileusousin eternal 22:5.

    Semantic categories: Human reign, Sin/grace as powers, Ironic Corinthian reign, Eschatological divine reign, Davidic reign

    Key verses: Rev 20:4, Rev 20:6, Rev 22:5, 1 Cor 15:25

    Relevance: Millennium = stage within eternal reign, not terminus.

    G4413 — protos¶

    Source: G4413-protos.md

    Occurrences: 105

    First, 105 occ (19 in Rev). Consistently marks sequence: first seal, trumpet, bowl. 2x2 ordinal matrix at 20:5-6.

    Semantic categories: Ordinal temporal, Rank/priority, Reversal formula, First/Last Adam, Alpha/Omega frame, Ordinal matrix, Seven firsts

    Key verses: Rev 20:5, Rev 20:6, Rev 21:1, Rev 8:7

    Relevance: Throughout Rev protos = sequence. Different-kind usage unprecedented.

    G4972 — sphragizo¶

    Source: G4972-sphragizo.md

    Occurrences: 28

    Seal, 28 occ (18 in Rev). Fifth confinement verb at 20:3. IC147 sealing chain.

    Semantic categories: Physical sealing, Divine authentication, Spiritual sealing, Eschatological marking, Concealing

    Key verses: Rev 20:3, Rev 7:3, Matt 27:66

    Relevance: esphragisen = fifth confinement verb. Connects to IC147.

    G2808 — kleio¶

    Source: G2808-kleio.md

    Occurrences: 19

    Shut, 15v/19tok (5 in Rev). Fourth confinement verb at 20:3. Structural bookends: 3:7-8, 21:25.

    Semantic categories: Physical doors, Exclusion, Secured space, Withholding, Open/shut authority

    Key verses: Rev 20:3, Rev 3:7-8, Rev 21:25

    Relevance: ekleisen = fourth confinement verb.

    G2902 — krateo¶

    Source: G2902-krateo.md

    Occurrences: 50

    Seize, 46v/50tok. First confinement verb at 20:2. Implies physical force.

    Semantic categories: Taking by hand, Arrest/seizure, Holding teaching, Holding fast, Restraint

    Key verses: Rev 20:2, Matt 14:3, Matt 26:50

    Relevance: ekratesen = first confinement verb. Physical force/seizure.

    G2362 — thronos¶

    Source: G2362-thronos.md

    Occurrences: 61

    Throne, 61 NT occ (46 in Rev = 75%). Great white throne 20:11 unique triple descriptor.

    Semantic categories: God throne, Messianic throne, Throne of grace, Judgment throne, Delegated thrones, Satan seat, Cosmic powers

    Key verses: Rev 20:4, Rev 20:11, Rev 4:2, Dan 7:9

    Relevance: megas leukos thronos unique. Dan 7:9 template distributed. VP165.

    G2288 — thanatos¶

    Source: G2288-thanatos.md

    Occurrences: 119

    Death, 106v/119tok. Personified at 20:13-14. Cast into lake of fire. Second death defined.

    Semantic categories: Physical death, Sin consequence, Reigning power, Christ atonement, Spiritual death, Personified/second death

    Key verses: Rev 20:13, Rev 20:14, Rev 6:8, 1 Cor 15:26

    Relevance: Active subject then passive patient at 20:13-14. Voice reversal.

    G1228 — diabolos¶

    Source: G1228-diabolos.md

    Occurrences: 38

    Devil/slanderer, 36v. Root: dia+ballo. Part of fourfold identification VP163.

    Semantic categories: Satan as tempter, Active adversary, Origin of sin, Eschatological judgment, Human slanderers

    Key verses: Rev 20:2, Rev 20:10, Rev 12:9, 1 Pet 5:8

    Relevance: Fourfold ID VP163. Root etymology: dia+ballo = one who throws across/accuses.

    G4567 — satanas¶

    Source: G4567-satanas.md

    Occurrences: 36

    Satan, 33v/36tok. Transliterated Hebrew title. Fourfold ID at 20:2.

    Semantic categories: Direct address, Conflict, Activity affecting persons, Deception/hindrance, Revelation usage

    Key verses: Rev 20:2, Rev 20:7, Rev 12:9

    Relevance: Fourfold ID VP163. Named at 20:2 and 20:7 framing millennium.

    G5550 — chronos¶

    Source: G5550-chronos.md

    Occurrences: 49

    Time/duration, 49v. mikron chronon at 20:3. Distinct from kairos.

    Semantic categories: Extended duration, Fulfilled time, Sovereign times, Pre-temporal, Brief remaining period

    Key verses: Rev 20:3, Rev 6:11, Rev 10:6

    Relevance: mikron chronon = measured duration. Different from mikron kairon at 12:12.

    G2919 — krino¶

    Source: G2919-krino.md

    Occurrences: 132

    Judge/decide, 98v/132tok. ekrithesan V-API-3P at 20:12-13.

    Semantic categories: Judicial verdict, Divine eschatological, Ongoing judgment, Interpersonal, Personal decision, Evaluating, Condemn/avenge

    Key verses: Rev 20:12, Rev 19:11, Acts 17:31

    Relevance: ekrithesan divine passive judgment. Completes judicial sequence.

    G975 — biblion¶

    Source: G975-biblion.md

    Occurrences: 32

    Scroll/book, 28v (18 in Rev). Two-book system at 20:12.

    Semantic categories: Sealed scroll, Book of life, Judgment records, Prophecy, Scripture, Written record, Legal document

    Key verses: Rev 20:12, Rev 5:1, Rev 13:8

    Relevance: biblia + allo biblion. allo = another of different kind.

    G1208 — deuteros¶

    Source: G1208-deuteros.md

    Occurrences: 47

    Second, 47 occ (12 in Rev). Second death at 2:11, 20:6, 20:14, 21:8.

    Semantic categories: Ordinal sequence, Second death, First/second pairing, Repetition, Second covenant

    Key verses: Rev 20:6, Rev 20:14, Rev 21:8, Rev 2:11

    Relevance: SP083 paired ordinals. First resurrection immunity from second death.

    G4442 — pyr¶

    Source: G4442-pyr.md

    Occurrences: 74

    Fire, 73v (25 in Rev = 34%). Fire from heaven 20:9; lake of fire 20:10,14,15.

    Semantic categories: Eschatological punishment, Judgment parables, Divine theophany, Cosmic plagues, Testing/refining

    Key verses: Rev 20:9, Rev 20:14, Ezek 38:22, Matt 25:41

    Relevance: ballo+pyr collocation 19x. Lake of fire = second death.

    G3173 — megas¶

    Source: G3173-megas.md

    Occurrences: 212

    Great, 200v (75 in Rev). thronon megan leukon 20:11 unique triple.

    Semantic categories: Physical size, Loud voice, Intensity, Importance, Natural upheaval, Apocalyptic scale

    Key verses: Rev 20:11, Rev 6:17, Rev 12:9

    Relevance: Unique triple descriptor at 20:11.

    G3528 — nikao¶

    Source: G3528-nikao.md

    Occurrences: 29

    Overcome/conquer, 28 occ (17 in Rev). Overcomer promises connect to millennium.

    Semantic categories: Eschatological victory, Cosmic victory of Christ, Moral victory, Military conquest, Forensic vindication

    Key verses: Rev 2:7, Rev 2:11, Rev 3:21, Rev 21:7

    Relevance: Rev 2:11 (not hurt by second death) -> Rev 20:6. Rev 3:21 (sit on throne) -> 20:4.

    G5507 — chilioi¶

    Source: G5507-chilioi.md

    Occurrences: 11

    Thousand, 11 occ (9 in Rev). chilia ete 6x in 20:2-7. TM174.

    Semantic categories: Millennium, Prophetic time periods, God timescale vs human

    Key verses: Rev 20:2, Rev 20:3, Rev 20:4, Rev 20:5, Rev 20:6, Rev 20:7

    Relevance: TM174: densest time-expression concentration. Uses etos not hemera/men/kairos.

    G2094 — etos¶

    Source: G2094-etos.md

    Occurrences: 49

    Year, 49 occ. Only prophetic period using etos = millennium.

    Semantic categories: Age/duration, Historical periods, Eschatological duration, Divine perspective, Liturgical cycle

    Key verses: Rev 20:2, Rev 20:4, Rev 20:6, 2 Pet 3:8

    Relevance: Vocabulary register distinction: symbolic periods use hemera/men/kairos, never etos.

    H8415 — tehom¶

    Source: H8415-thowm.md

    Occurrences: 42

    Deep/abyss, 35v/42tok. LXX = abyssos 30x (PMI 9.16). Gen 1:2 = primordial desolation.

    Semantic categories: Primordial deep, Subterranean water, Sea depths, Personified depth, Judgment waters

    Key verses: Gen 1:2, Gen 7:11, Ps 104:6

    Relevance: Primary OT source behind abyssos. Gen 1:2 tehom = formless desolation.

    H5375 — nasa¶

    Source: H5375-nasa.md

    Occurrences: 759

    Lift/bear/carry, 607v. Three registers: forgive, mediate, remove to desolation.

    Semantic categories: Lift up, Bear/carry, Take, Transport, Forgive, Accept, Exalt

    Key verses: Lev 16:22, Isa 53:12, Gen 4:13

    Relevance: Register 3 at Lev 16:22: carry to desolation, not atone.

    H4941 — mishpat¶

    Source: H4941-mishpat.md

    Occurrences: 448

    Judgment/justice, 406v. Primary OT of krima (175 LXX co-occ).

    Semantic categories: Judgment/verdict, Manner/custom, Right, Ordinance, Cause/sentence

    Key verses: Dan 7:22, Ps 89:14, Mic 6:8

    Relevance: Dan 7:22 parallel to Rev 20:4.

    G5001 — tagma¶

    Source: MISSING

    Occurrences: 1

    Order/rank hapax at 1 Cor 15:23. Military term for ordered sequence.

    Semantic categories: Military rank, Resurrection sequence

    Key verses: 1 Cor 15:23

    Relevance: tagmati hapax. Implies temporal gap between resurrection ranks.

    G86 — hades¶

    Source: MISSING

    Occurrences: 11

    Hades, 11 NT occ. Personified at 20:13-14 alongside thanatos.
