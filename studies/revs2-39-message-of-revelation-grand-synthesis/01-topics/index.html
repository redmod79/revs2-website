    Topics Research¶

    Question¶

    What is Revelation's complete theological message?

    REVELATION — General scriptures concerning¶

    • Exo 3:1-6,14
    • Exo 6:1-3
    • Exo 20-34
    • Lev 1-7
    • 1Ch 28:11-19
    • Mat 3:17
    • Mat 16:17
    • Mat 17:5

    God reveals himself to Moses EXO 3:1-6,14; 6:1-3. The law is revealed EXO 20-34; LEV 1-7. The pattern of the temple is revealed 1CH 28:11-19. The sonship of Jesus is revealed MAT 3:17; 16:17; 17:5. See INSPIRATION, PROPHECY, PROPHET, WORD OF GOD.

    WORSHIP — To be rendered only to God¶

    • Exo 20:3
    • Deu 5:7
    • Deu 6:13
    • Mat 4:10
    • Luk 4:8
    • Act 10:26
    • Act 14:15
    • Col 2:18
    • Rev 19:10
    • Rev 22:8

    To be rendered only to God EXO 20:3; DEU 5:7; 6:13; MAT 4:10; LUK 4:8; ACT 10:26; 14:15; COL 2:18; REV 19:10; 22:8. Of angels, forbidden REV 19:10; 22:8,9. Commanded GEN 35:1; EXO 23:17,18; REV 14:7; 19:10. Unclassified scriptures relating to worship include REV 11:1; 14:6,7; 15:4.

    SEAL — Figurative - certainty of divine approval¶

    • Jhn 6:27
    • Rom 15:28
    • 2Co 1:22
    • Eph 1:13
    • Eph 4:30
    • Rev 7:3
    • Rev 7:4
    • Dan 12:9
    • Rev 5:1

    A stamp used for signifying documents. Figurative: Of secrecy DAN 12:9; REV 5:1. Of certainty of divine approval JHN 6:27; ROM 15:28; 2CO 1:22; EPH 1:13; 4:30; REV 7:3,4. Circumcision was regarded as a seal of righteousness ROM 4:11.
