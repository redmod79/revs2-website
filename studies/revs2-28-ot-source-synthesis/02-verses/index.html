    Question¶

    How does Revelation use the Old Testament? What are the primary source books, their relative contributions, and the hierarchy of allusion types? Produce a source map, tally AN-tier allusions by OT book and strength, classify transformation types, and rank which OT books are most deeply embedded vs. superficially used.

    Song of Moses — verbatim in Rev 15:3-4 (AN044 Explicit)¶

    Then sang Moses and the children of Israel this song unto the LORD, and spake, saying, I will sing unto the LORD, for he hath triumphed gloriously: the horse and his rider hath he thrown into the sea. The LORD is my strength and song, and he is become my salvation: he is my God, and I will prepare him an habitation; my father's God, and I will exalt him. The LORD is a man of war: the LORD is his name.

    Context: Exo 15 — Israelites celebrate after Red Sea crossing; song of YHWH incomparable warrior

    Notes: mi khamocha incomparability formula; verbatim parallel to Rev 15:3-4; AN044 Explicit; establishes incomparability frame

    Mi Khamocha — incomparability formula echoed in Rev 13:4 and Rev 15:3¶

    Who is like unto thee, O LORD, among the gods? who is like thee, glorious in holiness, fearful in praises, doing wonders?

    Context: Exo 15 — Who is like thee among the gods?

    Notes: LXX tis homoios; beast parody at Rev 13:4 (tis homoios tō thēriō); AN081/AN159

    Earliest OT reign-forever declaration → Rev 11:15 (AN148)¶

    The LORD shall reign for ever and ever.

    Context: Exo 15 — The LORD shall reign for ever and ever

    Notes: Earliest OT declaration of eternal divine reign; picked up verbatim at Rev 11:15

    Royal Priesthood → Rev 1:6; 5:10 (AN008)¶

    And ye shall be unto me a kingdom of priests, and an holy nation. These are the words which thou shalt speak unto the children of Israel.

    Context: Exo 19 — Sinai covenant preamble; kingdom of priests formula

    Notes: basileian hiereis formula; AN008; Revelation assigns the status to all believers

    Sinai Theophany Formula — escalates at Rev 4:5, 8:5, 11:19, 16:18 (AN030, AN165)¶
