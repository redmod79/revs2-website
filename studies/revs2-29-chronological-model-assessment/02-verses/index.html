    Question¶

    After 28 studies cataloging 1,230 structural elements, what does the cumulative evidence say about Revelation's chronological structure?

    Theophany Formula Occurrence 1 — Three Elements (Lightnings, Thunderings, Voices)¶

    And out of the throne proceeded lightnings and thunderings and voices: and [there were] seven lamps of fire burning before the throne, which are the seven Spirits of God.

    Context: Throne room vision — the living creatures and seven lamps; baseline theophany formula

    Notes: SP054: 3 elements (lightnings, thunderings, voices). Baseline for escalation argument.

    Theophany Formula Occurrence 2 — Four Elements (Add Earthquake)¶

    And the angel took the censer, and filled it with fire of the altar, and cast [it] into the earth: and there were voices, and thunderings, and lightnings, and an earthquake.

    Context: 7th seal transition — incense offering, altar fire, then theophany formula with 4 elements

    Notes: SP054: 4 elements (lightnings, thunderings, voices, earthquake). One more than 4:5.
