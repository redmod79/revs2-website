    Question¶

    How does the Genesis-Revelation bookend structure work? Map the seven parallel pairs (tree of life, river, no more curse, face of God, very good/all things new, serpent, marriage) with LXX vocabulary chains. Does Matthew 24 provide a structural key to the seal sequence (Rev 6)? Does Paul's man of lawlessness (2 Thess 2:3-12) parallel Revelation's beast, using Daniel 7:25 as the common source?

    Prior Research Summary¶

    Six directly relevant prior studies identified. revs-44 (SP111 Strong) provides a 12-element Genesis-Revelation reversal catalog with VP documentation for each pair. revs-45 (SP114 Moderate) documents the 7-position Olivet Discourse / seal sequence parallel with vocabulary analysis at each position. revs-46 (SP115 Strong) establishes the 7-point 2 Thess 2 / Rev 13 functional parallel with Dan 7:25 verbatim LXX quotation (TM205: stoma laloun megala). revs2-08 documents the tree of life inclusio (Gen 2:9 LXX / Rev 2:7 / Rev 22:2,14 — exact xylon tes zoes phrase). revs2-22 establishes the naos = God/Lamb declaration (Rev 21:22) meaning the river in Rev 22:1 flows from whoever IS the temple. revs2-24 confirms the river source-shift (Ezek 47:1 threshold → Rev 22:1 throne) as AN094 Strong. Key open questions: LXX vocabulary chain verification for all 7 bookend pairs; katathema (G2652) vs. katara (G2671) distinction at Rev 22:3 vs. Gen 3:17; archaios (G0744) meaning in Rev 12:9 and 20:2; Luke 21:12 sequence deviation complicating SP114; naos question in 2 Thess 2:4 (church vs. physical temple).
