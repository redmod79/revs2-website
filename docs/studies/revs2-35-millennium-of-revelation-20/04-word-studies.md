# Word Studies

## Question
What does Revelation 20 say about the millennium — the binding of Satan, the first resurrection, the reign of saints, the release of Gog and Magog, and the great white throne judgment — and what do the text’s own structural, grammatical, and vocabulary indicators establish about the nature, sequence, and theological function of these events within Revelation’s macro-structure?

### G906 — ballo

**Source:** `G906-ballo.md`

**Occurrences:** 127

Cast/throw, 106v/127tok. 23 Rev occurrences trace Satan progressive defeat: heaven->earth->abyss->lake of fire.

**Semantic categories:** Throwing, Placing, Casting into judgment, Apocalyptic casting

**Key verses:** Rev 12:9, Rev 20:3, Rev 20:10, Rev 20:14

**Relevance:** Central ballo arc verb. 13 Rev occurrences = directional chronological trajectory.
