9. **Creation-domain targeting and the Rev 14:7 creation formula:**
READ $WORD_STUDIES/G1093-ge.md for the earth (ge) terminology. Mueller identifies a four-term creation formula in Rev 14:7: 'him that made heaven, and earth, and the sea, and the fountains of waters.' The four trumpet targets are: (1) earth/vegetation (ge, dendron, chortos), (2) sea (thalassa), (3) rivers and fountains (potamos, pege), (4) sun/moon/stars (helios, selene, aster). Compare these against Rev 14:7's formula — the correspondence is close but not exact (14:7 has 'heaven' where trumpets have 'sun-moon-stars'). For the Genesis 1 mapping: Day 3 produced dry land/earth (1:9-10) and vegetation (1:11-12), and also the gathering of waters/seas. Day 4 produced luminaries (1:14-18).
