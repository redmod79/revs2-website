## Question
What is the literary architecture and structural pattern system of the book of Revelation?

### G1096 — ginomai

**Source:** `D:/bible/bible-studies/word-studies/G1096-ginomai.md`

**Occurrences:** 702

Three-stage Ginomai arc as temporal backbone: egenomēn (aorist middle, self-initiated transport, V1/V2 en pneumati markers) → egeneto (proleptic aorist, 11:15, heaven declares as accomplished) → gegonen (perfect singular, 16:17, permanent consummation) → gegonan (perfect plural, 21:6, full completion). The arc is Revelation's built-in grammar for eschatological progression: initiated inauguration → declarative announcement → categorically final completion.

**Semantic categories:** temporal progression, eschatological completion, visionary transport, already/not-yet

**Key verses:** Rev 1:10, Rev 4:2, Rev 11:15, Rev 16:17, Rev 21:6

**Relevance:** Provides the grammatical backbone for both the fourfold en pneumati macro-division (egenomēn self-initiated vs. apēnegken transported) and the already/not-yet temporal framework (three-stage arc across the book's own grammar)
