8. **Telescoping vs. sequential vs. recapitulation at the seal-trumpet junction:**
Three competing models: (1) TELESCOPING — the 7th seal contains the trumpets; evidence: no closing formula separates seals from trumpets (TM058), 7th seal has no independent judgment content (TM055), 7th trumpet similarly lacks independent judgment content (VP070), theophany formula links judgment boundaries, Bauckham's 'encompasses.' (3) PROGRESSIVE RECAPITULATION — seals, trumpets, bowls cover the same period from different vantage points; evidence: Beale's 'silence has content,' shared themes across all three sequences, fraction escalation (1/4 → 1/3 → total) could indicate deepening description of the same events.
