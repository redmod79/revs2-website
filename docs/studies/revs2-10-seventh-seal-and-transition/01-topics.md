# Topics Research

## Question
What is the structural significance of the half-hour silence at the opening of the seventh seal (Rev 8:1)? How does this silence function as the transition between the seal sequence and the trumpet sequence? How does the incense prelude (Rev 8:2-5) — the angel with the golden censer, incense offered with the prayers of the saints, fire cast to earth — establish the sanctuary context for the trumpet judgments? Use Leviticus 16 and the Day of Atonement ritual to identify what sanctuary action Rev 8:3-5 depicts. Is the seventh seal the container for the trumpets (telescoping), or does it merely precede them (sequential)?
