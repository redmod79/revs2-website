# Cross-Testament Parallels

- Rev 17:1 → Jer 51:13 (OT, Many waters — geographic to universal symbol)
- Rev 17:4 → Rev 18:16 (NT, Garment description verbal parallel (gold, precious stones, pearls, purple))
- Rev 17:4 → Exod 28:5-6 (OT, Priestly-garment parody — matching elements minus blue)
- Rev 17:4 → Jer 51:7 (OT, Golden cup — agency shift from LORD's hand to Babylon's hand)
- Rev 17:5 → Exod 28:36 (OT, Forehead inscription inversion — HOLINESS TO THE LORD vs MYSTERY BABYLON)
- Rev 17:5 → 1 Ki 14:24 (OT, Abomination and prostitute vocabulary connection)
- Rev 17:6 → Matt 23:34-37 (NT, Blood of prophets — Jerusalem accused of prophet-killing)
- Rev 17:9 → Jer 51:25 (OT, Mountain = kingdom in prophetic imagery)
- Rev 17:9 → Dan 2:35 (OT, Stone becomes great mountain = God's kingdom)
- Rev 17:16 → Ezek 16:37-41 (OT, Harlot punishment template: strip, expose, burn with fire)
- Rev 17:16 → Dan 7:7 (OT, Ten horns, eating/consuming — Daniel's fourth beast)
- Rev 18:2 → Isa 21:9 (OT, Fall formula: epesen epesen / naphlah naphlah verbatim)
- Rev 18:2 → Jer 50:39-40 (OT, Permanent desolation — habitation of wild beasts/demons)
- Rev 18:4 → Jer 51:6,45 (OT, Come-out call: 'My people go ye out of the midst of her')
- Rev 18:4 → Isa 52:11 (OT, Six imperatives of separation — depart, go out, be clean)
- Rev 18:4 → Num 16:26 (OT, Depart from wicked — separation from rebellion within covenant community)
- Rev 18:7 → Isa 47:7-9 (OT, Queen boast and sudden widowhood — near-verbatim quotation)
- Rev 18:12-13 → Ezek 27:12-25 (OT, Cargo list comparison — Tyre oracle source for commercial profile)
- Rev 18:20 → Jer 51:48 (OT, Heaven rejoices over Babylon's fall)
- Rev 18:21 → Jer 51:63-64 (OT, Stone/millstone into water — Babylon shall sink forever)
- Rev 18:24 → Lam 4:13 (OT, Blood of prophets — shedding innocent blood)
- Rev 18:24 → Matt 23:34-37 (NT, Blood of prophets accusation — Jerusalem scope extended universally)
- Rev 17:1-3 → Rev 21:9-10 (NT, Babylon-bride mirror — identical angel introduction formula, apophero exclusive pairing)
- Rev 19:10 → Rev 22:8-9 (NT, Angel-worship refusal bracket closing Visions 3 and 4)
- Rev 17:8 → Rev 1:4,8 (NT, Beast existence parody vs divine existence formula)
- Rev 17:8 → John 17:12 (NT, Apoleia chain — beast and Judas share 'perdition' designation)
- Rev 17:5 → 2 Thess 2:3,7 (NT, Mystery of iniquity — son of perdition — apostasy connection)
- Rev 18:4 → John 10:16 (NT, Sheep in wrong fold (aule) but right flock (poimne))
- Rev 17:18 → Rev 11:8 (NT, he polis he megale — same phrase; 11:8 = Jerusalem; spiritual naming via pneumatikos)
