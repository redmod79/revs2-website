# Topics Research

## Question
Synthesizing all 25 prior revs2 studies, what is the macro-structure of the book of Revelation? How do the four en pneumati visions, the 1:19 threefold frame, the prologue-epilogue inclusio, the three judgment sequences with interludes, the sanctuary compartmental progression, the Babylon-bride mirror, the structural threads, the IC077 hybrid model, the overcomer arc, and the theophany formula progression combine into one coherent literary architecture?

### REVELATION — GENERAL

- Rev 1:1
- Rev 1:3
- Rev 4:1
- Rev 10:7
- Rev 22:6
- Rev 22:10

Revelation as unsealing of Daniel: Ha dei genesthai formula (Dan 2:28 → Rev 1:1 → 4:1 → 22:6) frames the book as Daniel's fulfilled prophecy. Rev 22:10 reverses Dan 12:4 (seal/do-not-seal contrast). Book's self-identification as 'apocalypsis' (G602: uncovering, disclosure) at 1:1. The time is at hand (ho kairos engus, 1:3/22:10 = TM017 inclusio).

Naos trajectory climax: God and Lamb ARE the naos (21:22, nominative predicate construction).

### GOLDEN ALTAR — GENERAL

- Rev 8:3
- Rev 9:13
- Rev 14:18

Two-altar grammar (revs2-22): chrysoun (golden) qualifier distinguishes HP golden altar (8:3, 9:13, 14:18) from OC bronze altar of martyrs (6:9, 16:7). The golden altar at 8:3-4 receives incense/prayers and transitions to the censer (8:5) at the seal-trumpet hinge. At 9:13 a voice from the golden altar releases the sixth trumpet (euphratean army). At 14:18 an angel 'who had power over fire' commands the grape harvest from the golden altar.
