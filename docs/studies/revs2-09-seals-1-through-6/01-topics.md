# Topics Research

## Question
What is the internal pattern of the four horsemen (seals 1-4)? Document the formula each horseman follows (living creature says 'Come,' horse appears, rider described, effect stated). How does the fifth seal's altar cry ('How long, O Lord?') introduce the theodicy question that drives the rest of the book? What role does the interlude between seals 6 and 7 (the 144,000 and the great multitude, Rev 7) play — how does it answer the question of 6:17 ('who shall be able to stand?')? What is the structural relationship between the seals and the Olivet Discourse (Matt 24)?

### SEAL — figurative and literal uses

Topic discovered via naves_semantic.py. Covers seal imagery in both testamental and apocalyptic contexts. Relevant entries cover the sealed scroll of Rev 5 and the sealing of the 144,000 in Rev 7.

### MARTYRDOM — general and instances

Topic discovered via naves_semantic.py. Covers instances of martyrdom from Abel through the apostles and into Revelation's seal and trumpet sequences.

### ALTAR — burnt offering and incense

Topic discovered via naves_semantic.py. Covers both the altar of burnt offering and the altar of incense. The distinction between the two altars is critical for the fifth seal spatial identification question.

### HORSE — symbolical uses

Topic discovered via naves_semantic.py. Covers both literal and symbolic horse references including the colored horses of Zechariah 1 and 6 and the four horsemen of Revelation 6.

### FAMINE — sent as judgment

Topic discovered via naves_semantic.py. Famine as divine judgment — part of the covenant-curse framework (Lev 26, Deut 28, Ezek 14:21). Corresponds to the third horseman (Rev 6:5-6) and the fourth horseman's killing means (Rev 6:8).

### VENGEANCE — belongs to God

Topic discovered via naves_semantic.py. Divine vengeance/vindication vocabulary. The fifth seal's ekdikeis (Rev 6:10) and its resolution at exedikesen (Rev 19:2) trace through this topic.

### PERSECUTION — of the righteous

Topic discovered via naves_semantic.py. Persecution of the righteous — covers the fifth seal martyrs and the Olivet Discourse parallel at Matt 24:9.

### EARTHQUAKES — symbolical

Topic discovered via naves_semantic.py. Earthquake (seismos) imagery in eschatological contexts. Rev 6:12 (sixth seal), 8:5 (trumpet introduction), 11:13 (trumpet), 16:18 (bowl climax).

### JUDGMENT — the general

Topic discovered via naves_semantic.py. General judgment — covers the sealed scroll as containing God's judgments and the horsemen as executing judgment.
