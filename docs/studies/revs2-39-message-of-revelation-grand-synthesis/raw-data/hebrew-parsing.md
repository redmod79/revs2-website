# Raw Hebrew Parsing Output

## Dan 7:17

Aramaic Text: אִלֵּין חֵיוָתָא רַבְרְבָתָא דִּי אִנִּין אַרְבַּע אַרְבְּעָה מַלְכִין יְקוּמוּן מִן־אַרְעָא

Key forms:
- אִלֵּין (illein): Demonstrative Pronoun plural — 'these'
- חֵיוָתָא (cheywata): Noun feminine plural — 'beasts/animals' (Aramaic equivalent of Hebrew chayyah)
- רַבְרְבָתָא (rabrevatah): Adjective feminine plural — 'great' (intensive/reduplicated form)
- מַלְכִין (malkin): Noun masculine plural absolute — 'kings' (= kingdoms, as Dan 7:23 clarifies: 'the fourth beast shall be a fourth kingdom')
- יְקוּמוּן (yequmun): Verb Peal Imperfect 3mp of qum — 'shall arise/stand up'
- מִן־אַרְעָא (min-ar'a): Preposition + Noun feminine singular — 'from the earth'

Note: malkin (kings) functions as metonymy for malkuwan (kingdoms), confirmed by v.23 where the angel explicitly decodes: 'the fourth beast shall be a fourth KINGDOM (malku) upon earth.' This is the explicit decoder for Revelation 13's composite beast.
