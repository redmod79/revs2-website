# Word Studies

## Question
What is Revelation's complete theological message?

### G4352 — proskyneo

**Source:** `D:\bible\bible-studies\word-studies\G4352-proskyneo.md`

**Occurrences:** 65

Central worship verb appearing 22+ times in Revelation. The linguistic axis of the entire allegiance conflict — the exact same word applies to both Creator-worship (14:7) and beast-worship (13:4,8,12,15). Properly means to kiss the hand toward, to prostrate in reverence. LXX equivalent of shachah H7812.

**Semantic categories:** worship, reverence, prostration, allegiance

**Key verses:** Rev 14:7, Rev 13:4, Rev 13:8, Rev 13:12, Rev 13:15, Rev 19:10, Rev 22:8-9

**Relevance:** Same word for beast-worship and Creator-worship creates binary with no neutral vocabulary — the allegiance conflict that drives the entire book.

### G5480 — charagma

**Source:** `D:\bible\bible-studies\word-studies\G5480-charagma.md`

**Occurrences:** 9

Beast's mark — 8 of 9 NT occurrences in Revelation. No LXX antecedent; purpose-built anti-symbol counterfeiting the divine seal (sphragis). Appears on forehead OR hand (e disjunction) — asymmetric with God's seal (forehead only). Part of the inseparable worship-image-mark triad.

**Semantic categories:** mark, brand, imprint, allegiance marker

**Key verses:** Rev 13:16-17, Rev 14:9, Rev 14:11, Rev 16:2, Rev 19:20, Rev 20:4

**Relevance:** Counterfeit of sphragis. God requires conviction (forehead only); the beast accepts mere compliance (forehead OR hand). Mutually exclusive allegiance markers.
