# Word Studies

## Question
What does structural and OT-typological evidence identify about the beast of Revelation 13? Evaluate four models: individual eschatological figure, political-religious system, symbolic-universal, preterist.

### G2342 — therion

**Occurrences:** 46

Beast/wild animal. Diminutive of ther.

**Relevance:** Central noun — the beast itself. Rev 13 is built around therion.

---

### G988 — blasphemia

**Occurrences:** 19

Blasphemy/slander/evil speaking

**Relevance:** Beast's defining activity — blasphemia against God's name, tabernacle, heaven-dwellers (13:6 threefold target).

---
