# Topics Research

## Question
SHORT STUDY: What does IC077 mean for readers? How do the three judgment sequences (seals, trumpets, bowls) relate? This synthesizes established Part 1 findings — NOT a re-investigation.

### JUDGMENTS — Divine

- Ezek 14:21
- Rev 15:1
- Rev 16:1-21

Ezekiel 14:21 four sore judgments framework (sword, famine, beast, pestilence); Revelation's three judgment sequences escalate from permitted consequences through warnings to execution

### TRUMPET — Prophetic/Warning

- Num 10:1-10
- Joel 2:1
- Rev 8:2-6

OT trumpet tradition: alarm/warning function (Num 10:9 'blow an alarm... ye shall be remembered before the LORD'); Joel 2:1 'Blow ye the trumpet in Zion, and sound an alarm in my holy mountain'; Rev 8:2 seven angels with seven trumpets

### SEAL — Prophetic

- Rev 6:1-17
- Rev 8:1

Seven seals opened by the Lamb; horsemen receive power through divine passive edothe; scope limited to 1/4 (Rev 6:8)

### PLAGUE — Divine Judgment

- Rev 15:1
- Rev 15:6
- Rev 15:8
- Rev 16:9
- Rev 16:21

Seven last plagues (eschatas plegas); 'in them is filled up the wrath of God' (Rev 15:1); plague vocabulary connects to Exodus plague tradition

### TEMPLE — Heavenly Sanctuary

- Rev 11:19
- Rev 15:5-8
- Rev 21:22

Naos opened at Rev 11:19 (ark visible); naos filled with smoke at Rev 15:8 (access barred); naos absent in New Jerusalem (Rev 21:22 'the Lord God Almighty and the Lamb are the temple')

### ALTAR — Heavenly

- Rev 6:9
- Rev 8:3-5
- Rev 9:13
- Rev 14:18
- Rev 16:7
- Rev 19:2

Altar vindication arc: passive reception of martyrs (6:9) -> mediating incense (8:3-5) -> commanding voice (9:13) -> authority over fire (14:18) -> personified speech (16:7) -> resolved vindication (19:2)

### INTERCESSION — Heavenly

- Rev 8:3-4
- Rev 15:8

Intercession active at Rev 8:3-4 (incense offered with prayers on golden altar); intercession absent at Rev 15:8 (no man able to enter temple); the censer (libanotos) never appears after Rev 8:5

### WRATH — Divine

- Rev 6:16-17
- Rev 11:18
- Rev 14:10
- Rev 15:1
- Rev 15:7
- Rev 16:1
- Rev 16:19
- Rev 19:15

Wrath vocabulary: orge (settled judicial wrath) at Rev 6:17 and 11:18 (identical elthen he orge formula); thumos (passionate fury) concentrated in bowl judgments; dual vocabulary combined at Rev 16:19 and 19:15 for maximal intensity

### ATONEMENT, DAY OF — Typology

- Lev 16:2-17
- Rev 15:5-8

Five DOA indicators converge at Rev 15:5-8: triple-genitive sanctuary identification (NT hapax), priestly DOA vestments, smoke filling naos, universal exclusion clause, temporal until clause; all parallel Lev 16

### INCENSE — Heavenly

- Rev 5:8
- Rev 8:3-4
- Rev 18:13

Thymiama (incense) = prayers of saints at Rev 5:8; offered with prayers at Rev 8:3-4; phonetic near-identity with thymos (wrath) marks the vessel transformation arc
