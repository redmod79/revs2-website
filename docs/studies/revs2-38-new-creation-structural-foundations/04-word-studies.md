# Word Studies

## Question
What does Revelation's literary architecture establish about the new creation (Rev 21:1-22:5)?

### G2537 — kainos

**Occurrences:** 44

New in quality/character. 44 NT tokens, 38 unique verses, 14 books. 7 Revelation occurrences (2:17, 3:12, 5:9, 14:3, 21:1, 21:2, 21:5). Qualitative newness vs neos temporal newness. LXX bridge from H2319 chadash with 38 co-occurrences (PMI 32.00). Central to new creation vocabulary.

---

### G3501 — neos

**Occurrences:** 24

New in time/age, young. 24 tokens, 20 unique verses, 11 books. ABSENT from Revelation entirely — negative evidence confirming kainos selection is deliberate. LXX renders H2319 chadash primarily as kainos (38x), only 3x as neos.

---

### G3485 — naos

**Occurrences:** 46

Inner shrine/temple. 46 occurrences, concentrated in Revelation (16 uses, 37% of all). Exclusive term in Revelation — hieron (G2411) never used. 16 occurrences trace compartmental progression through chapters 3-16, then negation at 21:22 where God and the Lamb ARE the naos. LXX bridge from H1964 heykal (PMI 30.9).

---

### G4633 — skene

**Occurrences:** 20

Tent/tabernacle. 20 occurrences. Hebrews (10), Rev (3), Acts (3). Rev 21:3 skene tou theou — God's eschatological dwelling. LXX bridge from H168 ohel (230x), H4908 mishkan (88x), H5521 sukkah (20x).

---

### G4637 — skenoo

**Occurrences:** 5

To tabernacle/dwell. 5 occurrences, ALL Johannine. John 1:14 eskenosen (aorist — incarnation), Rev 7:15 skenosei (future — proleptic), Rev 12:12 skenountes (present — heavenly), Rev 13:6 skenountas (present — blasphemed), Rev 21:3 skenosei (future — permanent). Incarnation-to-consummation arc.

---

### G4636 — skenos

**Occurrences:** 2

Tabernacle (body metaphor). 2 occurrences, both 2 Cor 5:1,4. Exclusively Pauline. Body as fragile tent. Not directly used in Revelation.

---

### G3586 — xylon

**Occurrences:** 19

Tree/wood. 19 tokens, 17 verses, 8 books. Rev: 4 occurrences (2:7, 22:2, 22:14, 18:12). Tree of life (xylon tes zoes) = verbatim LXX Gen 2:9. Also cross-connection via Gal 3:13 and Acts crucifixion sermons. LXX bridge from H6086 ets (219 co-occurrences, PMI 33.69).

---

### G2652 — katathema

**Occurrences:** 1

Curse. NT HAPAX at Rev 22:3. kata + anathema compound. Terminal reversal of Gen 3:17 epikataratos. Not found in LXX — exclusively NT. The event it describes is unique: pan katathema ouk estai eti.

---

### G2671 — katara

**Occurrences:** 6

Curse/imprecation. 6 tokens, 5 verses, 4 books. Gal 3:13 Christ redeemed us from the katara. Intermediate stage between Gen 3:17 and Rev 22:3. LXX bridge from H7045 qelalah (26 co-occurrences, PMI 29.93).

---

### G1096 — ginomai

**Occurrences:** 702

Become/come to be. 668 tokens, 631 verses. Ginomai arc in Revelation: egeneto (11:15 proleptic aorist) → gegonen (16:17 perfect singular) → gegonan (21:6 perfect plural). Tracks eschatological realization through progressive perfect grammar.

---

### G2816 — kleronomeo

**Occurrences:** 22

Inherit. 22 tokens, 17 verses, 8 books. Rev 21:7 capstone: ho nikon kleronomeo tauta panta. Verb shift from doso (letters) to kleronomeo. Future indicative dominates (56%). LXX bridge from H3423 yarash (100 co-occurrences).

---

### G3820 — palaios

**Occurrences:** 19

Old/antiquated. 19 tokens, 15 verses, 9 books. Antonym pair with kainos (6x collocations). The old passes away so the new can emerge. Absent from Revelation — the old is already gone.

---

### G3565 — nymphe

**Occurrences:** 8

Bride/daughter-in-law. 8 tokens, 7 verses, 4 books. Rev: 4 occurrences (18:23, 21:2, 21:9, 22:17). City-bride dual identity. LXX bridge from H3618 kallah (28 co-occurrences).

---

### G4160 — poieo

**Occurrences:** 597

Make/do. 597 tokens, broadest Greek action verb. Rev 21:5 kaina poio panta — PRESENT tense, ongoing creative act. 26 Revelation occurrences.

---

### H2319 — chadash

**Occurrences:** 53

New/fresh. 53 tokens, 48 verses, 20 books. Isa 65:17 bara shamayim chadashim. LXX → kainos G2537 38/48 times (PMI 8.730). OT source vocabulary for new creation.

---

### H1254 — bara

**Occurrences:** 59

Create (divine). 59 tokens, 46 verses. Isa 65:17 bara (divine creation verb). Connects original creation (Gen 1:1) to new creation (Isa 65:17). Qal dominates (38/54).

---

### H779 — arar

**Occurrences:** 66

Execrate/curse. 66 tokens, 52 verses, 12 books. Gen 3:14,17 arur formulas. Qal participle arur dominates (~65%). LXX → epikataratos G1944 (35 co-occurrences, PMI 8.69). Root of curse chain reversed at Rev 22:3.

---

### H6086 — ets

**Occurrences:** 365

Tree/wood. 365 tokens, 288 verses, 30 books. Gen 2:9 ets hayyim (tree of life). LXX → xylon G3586 (219 co-occurrences, PMI 6.25). Hebrew source bridging to xylon tes zoes in LXX and Rev 22:2.

---

### H7931 — shakan

**Occurrences:** 150

Dwell/tabernacle. 150 tokens, 123 verses, 23 books. Source of Shekinah concept and mishkan (tabernacle). Phonetic pathway: shakan → Shekinah → skene/skenoo. LXX → kataskenoo G2681 (46 co-occurrences, PMI 7.37).

---

### H5521 — sukkah

**Occurrences:** 34

Booth/tabernacle. 34 tokens, 29 verses, 14 books. Feast of Tabernacles vocabulary. LXX → skene G4633 (20 co-occurrences, PMI 5.58).

---
