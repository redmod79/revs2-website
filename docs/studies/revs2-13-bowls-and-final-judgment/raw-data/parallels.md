# Cross-Testament Parallels

- Rev 15:8 → Lev 16:17 (ot, vocabulary + structural — exclusion from sanctuary during ritual action with temporal limit (VP097))
- Rev 15:8 → Exo 40:34-35 (ot, vocabulary — glory/smoke filling, inability to enter; but lacks temporal 'until' clause and is inauguration context)
- Rev 15:8 → Isa 6:4 (ot, vocabulary — smoke fills temple; but lacks exclusion clause)
- Rev 15:3 → Exo 15:1 (ot, vocabulary + structural — Song of Moses, victory, divine works (VP106, Strong, 5 links))
- Rev 15:3 → Deut 32:4 (ot, vocabulary — 'just and true are thy ways' (VP107, Strong, 5 links))
- Rev 16:2 → Exo 9:8-11 (ot, vocabulary — LXX helkos (G1668) for shechyin (H7822) boils plague)
- Rev 16:3-4 → Exo 7:19-20 (ot, vocabulary — haima (blood), water-to-blood plague)
- Rev 16:10 → Exo 10:21-23 (ot, vocabulary — skotos/skotoo darkness plague)
- Rev 16:21 → Exo 9:22-25 (ot, vocabulary — chalaza (G5464) / barad (H1259) hail plague (PMI 29.60))
- Rev 16:18-21 → Ezek 38:19-22 (ot, vocabulary + structural — earthquake, mountains, hailstones, fire, nations (AN052, Strong, 5 elements))
- Rev 16:19 → Jer 25:15-16 (ot, vocabulary — cup of wrath/fury (thumos/chemah) given to nations)
- Rev 16:15 → Rev 3:3 (nt, vocabulary + structural — thief imagery, watchfulness warning, same author (score 0.436))
- Rev 16:15 → 1 Thess 5:2-4 (nt, vocabulary — Day of Lord as thief, pre-parousia warning)
- Rev 16:15 → Matt 24:43 (nt, vocabulary — original thief parable source, watchfulness)
- Rev 16:15 → 2 Pet 3:10 (nt, vocabulary — Day of Lord as thief, cosmic dissolution)
- Rev 16:17 → John 19:30 (nt, structural — completion declarations; different verbs (ginomai vs. teleo) at different nodes (SP048))
- Rev 16:17 → Rev 21:6 (nt, vocabulary + structural — same verb ginomai, singular (16:17) vs. plural (21:6), judgment vs. new creation)
- Rev 16:13-14 → Rev 14:6-9 (nt, structural — three entities, opposite functions (truth vs. deception), counterfeit inversion)
- Rev 15:6 → Lev 16:4 (ot, vocabulary — linen garments, girdle, priestly context, sanctuary ritual (VP108))
- Rev 15:7 → Rev 5:8 (nt, vocabulary + structural — phialas chrysas gemousas + genitive content; vessel transformation (SP119))
- Rev 16:12 → Isa 44:27-45:1 (ot, structural — drying of waters to conquer Babylon (Cyrus typology))
