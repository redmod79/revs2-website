# Topics Research

## Question
What are the primary structural markers in the book of Revelation? Trace the four "in the Spirit" (en pneumati) markers at Rev 1:10, 4:2, 17:3, and 21:10 — how do they divide the book into four major visions? Trace the "and I saw" (kai eidon) transitions — where do they occur, what do they introduce, and how do they subdivide the visions into scenes? Trace the "after these things" (meta tauta) indicators. How do these three marker systems work together to create Revelation's structural skeleton? For each marker, document the Greek form, every occurrence, and what structural boundary it signals. Note where the markers' structural signals converge and where they create ambiguity.

General prophecy references. Revelation self-identifies as prophecy (Rev 1:3, 22:7, 22:10, 22:18-19). The vision-report genre connects to the broader prophetic tradition.

### REVELATION — General — God reveals himself

- Rev 1:1
- Dan 2:22
- Dan 2:28
- Amos 3:7
- Matt 11:25
- 1 Cor 2:10

God reveals himself and his purposes. Revelation 1:1 uses apokalypsis (unveiling) as the book's title — a structural signal that the entire book is a single act of divine disclosure.

### TRANCE — General

- Num 24:4
- Acts 10:10
- Acts 22:17

Trance-like visionary states parallel the en pneumati formula. Balaam fell into a trance (Num 24:4). Peter fell into a trance (Acts 10:10). Paul in a trance (Acts 22:17).

### EZEKIEL — Visions of

- Ezek 1:3
- Ezek 2:9
- Ezek 3:12-14
- Ezek 8:3
- Ezek 10:1-7
- Ezek 11:24
- Ezek 37:1-14
- Ezek 40:2
- Ezek 43:5

Ezekiel's visions provide the primary OT model for Revelation's en pneumati/apophero pattern. Spirit-transport passages: Ezek 3:12-14, 8:3, 11:24, 37:1, 40:2, 43:5. Each involves the Spirit lifting/carrying the prophet to a specific location for a vision.

### EARTHQUAKES — Symbolical

- Rev 6:12
- Rev 8:5
- Rev 11:13
- Rev 11:19
- Rev 16:18

Earthquakes (seismos G4578) in Revelation function as structural markers within the Sinai-theophany formula. Progressive intensification: absent at 4:5, present at 8:5, 11:19, and 16:18 with superlatives.

### LIGHTNING — General scriptures

- Rev 8:5

Lightning (astrape G796) as theophanic manifestation. Present at all four Sinai-theophany formula points in Revelation. OT source: Exo 19:16.

### SINAI — Law delivered at

- Exo 19:16-18
- Exo 20:18
- Deut 4:11-12
- Heb 12:18-21

The Sinai theophany (thunders, lightnings, voice of trumpet, earthquake) is the OT source for Revelation's theophany formula at 4:5, 8:5, 11:19, 16:18.

### HOLY SPIRIT — General scriptures

- Matt 3:16
- Acts 2:4
- Rom 8:9
- 1 Cor 2:10
- Rev 1:10
- Rev 2:7
- Rev 4:2
- Rev 14:13

The Holy Spirit (pneuma G4151) appears in Revelation both as the agent of visionary transport (en pneumati, anarthrous) and as the speaking Spirit (to pneuma, articular). The distinction between anarthrous and articular pneuma is structurally significant.

### BABYLON — Figurative

- Rev 14:8
- Rev 16:19
- Rev 17:5
- Rev 18:2
- Rev 18:10
- Rev 18:21

Babylon as the harlot city — structurally paired with New Jerusalem as the bride city. The two-cities parallel (17:1-3 / 21:9-10) is the strongest verbal parallel in Revelation.
