# Cross-Testament Parallels

- Rev 13:11 → Dan 7:8 (ot, vocabulary + structural — another horn/beast rising among established powers)
- Rev 13:11 → Matt 7:15 (nt, thematic — appearance vs. reality (lamb/sheep exterior, dragon/wolf interior))
- Rev 13:13 → 1 Kings 18:38 (ot, vocabulary + typological — fire from heaven authenticating worship (true vs. counterfeit))
- Rev 13:13 → Acts 2:3 (nt, vocabulary + structural — fire from heaven as Spirit authentication (genuine vs. counterfeit))
- Rev 13:14-15 → Dan 3:1-7 (ot, structural + vocabulary — image created, worship commanded, death decree, universal scope; tselem/eikon LXX parallel)
- Rev 13:14 → 2 Thess 2:9-10 (nt, vocabulary — semeia, planao/apate, pseudous/pseudoprophetes; satanic working parallel)
- Rev 13:15 → Ps 135:15-17 (ot, inversion — OT idols have no breath; beast image receives pneuma)
- Rev 13:15 → Hab 2:18-19 (ot, inversion — graven image has no breath; beast image receives pneuma and speaks)
- Rev 13:15 → Ezek 37:9 (ot, typological inversion — God's ruach gives genuine life to dry bones; earth beast gives counterfeit pneuma)
- Rev 13:15 → Gen 2:7 (ot, typological — Creator breathes life; earth beast counterfeits creative act with derived permission)
- Rev 13:12 → John 16:14 (nt, structural parody — Spirit glorifies Christ; earth beast glorifies/causes worship of first beast)
- Rev 13:16 → Eph 1:13 (nt, structural parody — Spirit seals believers (sphragis); earth beast marks followers (charagma))
- Rev 13:1 → Dan 7:2-3 (ot, vocabulary + structural — beasts rising from the sea; prophetic beast convention)
- Rev 13:5 → Dan 7:8 (ot, vocabulary — stoma laloun megala verbatim from Dan 7:8 LXX)
- Rev 13:14 → Gen 1:26-27 (ot, vocabulary — eikon chain: original imago Dei → beast counterfeit)
- Rev 14:7 → Exod 20:11 (ot, vocabulary — heaven, earth, sea creation formula; Creator-worship counterprogramming beast worship)
- Rev 13:16 → Rom 4:11 (nt, structural — sign-seal precedent: circumcision as sphragis of righteousness; sign can function as seal)
- Rev 16:13 → Acts 2:3-4 (nt, structural parody — Pentecost tongues/fire → three unclean spirits from mouths; triple-Pentecost counterfeit)
- Rev 13:11 → Rev 17:15 (nt, interpretive key — sea = peoples/nations; earth = contrasting origin (newer, less populated territory))
- Rev 19:20 → Rev 13:14 (nt, verbal chain — signs done enopion autou + planao/eplanesen confirming earth beast = false prophet)
- Rev 13:11 → Rev 5:6 (nt, contrast — Christ-Lamb with 7 horns vs. earth beast with 2 horns like a lamb)
- Rev 13:14 → Matt 24:24 (nt, vocabulary — pseudoprophetai + semeia megala + planao; Olivet Discourse parallel)
- Rev 13:14 → Col 1:15 (nt, vocabulary — eikon chain: Christ as authentic eikon of God vs. manufactured beast eikon)
- Rev 12:9 → Rev 13:14 (nt, vocabulary — planao: dragon deceives whole world → earth beast deceives earth-dwellers (inherited function))
- Rev 13:1 → Isa 27:1 (ot, vocabulary + thematic — sea as chaos/dragon domain; leviathan in the sea)
