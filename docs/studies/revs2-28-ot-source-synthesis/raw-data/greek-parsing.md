Key terms: ἐλαῖαι (G1636) N-NPF olive trees [feminine]; λυχνίαι (G3087) N-NPF lampstands [feminine]; ἑστῶτες (G2476) V-RAP-NPM Perf Act Ptcp Nom Pl M — masculine participle. Constructio ad sensum: grammatically plural feminine subjects (olive trees + lampstands) take masculine participle ἑστῶτες — signals these are PERSONS (the two witnesses), not inanimate objects. Merger: Zech 4 had separate olive trees and separate lampstand; Rev 11:4 fuses them into single identity.
