## Dan 7:9

Aramaic (not Hebrew): חָזֵ֣ה הֲוֵ֗ית עַ֣ד דִּ֤י כָרְסָוָן֙ רְמִ֔יו וְעַתִּ֥יק יֹומִ֖ין יְתִ֑ב לְבוּשֵׁ֣הּ כִּתְלַ֣ג חִוָּ֗ר. Key terms: כָרְסָוָן (karsawan) thrones Noun mp Abs; עַתִּ֥יק יֹומִ֖ין (attiq yomin) Ancient of Days [attiq = old/ancient]; יְתִ֑ב (yetiv) peal Perf 3ms 'sat/was seated'; תְלַ֣ג חִוָּ֗ר (telag chivvar) snow white; עֲמַ֣ר נְקֵ֔א (amar neqa) pure wool. Ancient of Days throne imagery is distributed in Revelation: white-wool hair applied to Son of Man at Rev 1:14 (Christological merger); throne-setting at Rev 4-5; fiery wheels → sea of glass (repositioned); books opened at Rev 20:11-12.

## Exo 15:11

Hebrew: מִֽי־כָמֹ֤כָה בָּֽאֵלִם֙ יְהוָ֔ה מִ֥י כָּמֹ֖כָה נֶאְדָּ֣ר בַּקֹּ֑דֶשׁ נֹורָ֥א תְהִלֹּ֖ת עֹ֥שֵׂה פֶֽלֶא. Key terms: מִי (mi) IntPron 'who'; כָמֹ֤כָה (khamocha) Prep+2ms 'like you'; אֵלִם (elim) gods/mighty ones; נֶאְדָּ֣ר (neʾdar) Niphal Ptcp 'glorious/awesome'; קֹּ֑דֶשׁ (qodesh) holiness; נֹורָ֥א (nora) Niphal Ptcp 'fearful/awesome'; פֶֽלֶא (pele) miracle/wonder. The mi khamocha formula (who is like you?) is the classic OT incomparability declaration. LXX renders: tis homoios soi en theois Kyrie. Rev 15:4 preserves tis (who) in the rhetorical opening; Rev 13:4 inverts it as beast-parody: tis homoios tō thēriō (who is like the beast?).

## Ezek 47:1

Hebrew: וַיְשִׁבֵנִי֮ אֶל־פֶּ֣תַח הַבַּיִת֒ וְהִנֵּה־מַ֣יִם יֹצְאִ֗ים מִתַּ֨חַת מִפְתַּ֤ן הַבַּ֨יִת֙ קָדִ֔ימָה. Key terms: פֶּ֣תַח (petach) opening/door; מִפְתַּ֤ן (miphtan) Noun ms Cst 'threshold/podium' — threshold of the house as water source; מַ֣יִם (mayim) water; יֹצְאִ֗ים (yotsaim) Qal Ptcp mp 'going out'; קָדִ֔ימָה (qadimah) eastward. The miphtan (threshold) is the specific source point. Rev 22:1 source-shift: from temple threshold (Ezek) → throne of God and Lamb (Rev) — explained by Rev 21:22 (God IS the temple, so throne = threshold).
