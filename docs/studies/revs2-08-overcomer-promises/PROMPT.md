5. **The naos tension: pillar in temple (3:12) vs. no temple therein (21:22):**
READ `$WORD_STUDIES/G3485-naos.md`. The naos progression documented in prior studies: naos present/active in Rev 3-11 (3:12; 7:15; 11:1-2,19) → naos filled/inaccessible at 15:8 ('no man was able to enter into the naos') → naos absent as building at 21:22 ('I saw no naos therein: for the Lord God Almighty and the Lamb ARE the naos of it'). The cubic New Jerusalem (21:16, equal length/breadth/height) structurally IS an expanded Most Holy Place (1 Kgs 6:20, 20x20x20 cubits). If the city IS the temple, the pillar promise is fulfilled in the city itself.
