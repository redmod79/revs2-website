# Topics Research

## Question
What is the structural significance of Revelation's time periods? Why does the 3.5-year period appear in three different expressions — 42 months (Rev 11:2, 13:5), 1,260 days (Rev 11:3, 12:6), and 'a time, and times, and half a time' (Rev 12:14)? Use Daniel 7:25 and 12:7 as the source texts. Do the three expressions map to different subjects or perspectives? What does the text of Revelation 20 explicitly state about the millennium — what happens before, during, and after the thousand years? Document the sequence of events in Rev 20:1-15 without importing external frameworks.
